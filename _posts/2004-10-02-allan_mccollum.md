<p>I didn't manage to get to Allan McCollum's show at <a href="http://www.petzel.com/index_mccollum.html">Friedrich Petzel</a> until this afternoon, and now it's gone.  But I liked the two installations too much to let a little matter of their deposition stop me from showing this installation photograph and from linking to both the gallery's pages and <a href="http://home.att.net/~allanmcnyc/">his</a> own site for more images and information about this wonderful artist.</p>

<p>At least we have five more months to see his <a href="http://home.att.net/~artarchives/paf/pvpaf.html">"Three Perfect Vehicles"</a> currently installed at the southeast entrance to Central Park.  This time I promise myself I won't wait until the last day.</p>
