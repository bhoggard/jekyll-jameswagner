Jim Hodges <em>Look and See</em> painted stainless steel 11.5'x 50' x 1"  [detail of installation]</p>

<p>It appears light as a feather in spite of its medium, but I think <a href="http://www.crggallery.com/artist_pages/hodges_jim/hodges_jim.htm">Jim Hodges</a>'s enormous sculpture is more effective when viewed close up, at least while it's located in its current visually-busy <a href="http://www.creativetime.org/programs/archive/artontheplaza/artists/hodges/index.php">environment</a>.  Yes, I know it was supposedly created for the space at the base of this hotel, but I'd love to see it in a large, active public plaza.</p>
