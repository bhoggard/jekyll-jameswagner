<p>Are there really <a href="http://www.commondreams.org/views02/0906-06.htm">people out there</a>?  Maybe there <i>is</i> still hope, but we're going to have to let each other know we're here.  Don't hide under a basket!</p>

<blockquote>
<p>If you scratch the surface of the poll numbers about Bush and Ashcroft's overwhelming support, you get down to a lot of people with a lot of questions. Some of them are afraid that they are alone in what they are thinking. What it takes to get them excited and to get them involved is for them to see someone standing up so that they will know they are not alone."</p>
</blockquote>

<p>A member of a Madison, Wisconsin, school board who took a supposedly very unpopular position and survived attests that Americans are ready for the debate being denied us by Washington.</p>

<blockquote>
<p>"If the last year taught us anything, it's this: Yes, of course, if you step out of the mainstream you will get called names and threatened. But you will also discover that a lot of Americans still recognize that dissenters are the real defenders of freedom."</p>
</blockquote>
