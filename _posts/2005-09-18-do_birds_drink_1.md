<p>I was walking with Barry and some friends along 11th Avenue just above 24th Street when I spotted a birdhouse shape on the far side of a tree [by coincidence one of my most favorite trees in the entire city].  I went to investigate and discovered the entrance hole blocked by something constructed of wood and painted black.  Concerned for any potential occupants, I poked the obstruction with the end of my umbrella and what looked like water flowed out of the perch/spigot below.  The "water" turned out to be vodka.</p>

<p>I don't know this fairytale at all.</p>
