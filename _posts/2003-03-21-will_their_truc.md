<p>Yesterday in the <u>Daily</u> <u>News</u> "Rush & Molloy" included a small item reporting that Cadillac, which is the <a href="http://www.miami.com/mld/miamiherald/business/5382464.htm?template=contentModules/printstory.jsp">exclusive</a> provider of cars for the Academy Awards this year, offering attendees the choice of a standard sedans or a monster truck, is having an unexpected <a href="http://www.nydailynews.com/news/col/story/68664p-63905c.html">run</a> on their would-be tank, the Escalade, "supposedly because rolling up in limos looks tackier."</p>

<p>Huh?  If they're trying to be sensitive, although I <a href="https://jameswagner.com/index.php?m=200303#770">don't know why</a> we should really think they are, someone should tell the stars that the suffering people of Iraq know that there's nothing tackier than Americans, especially packs of Americans, in gas-guzzling SUVs trying to imitate the Hummers of Yankee, oil-field-conquering invaders.</p>
