<p><strong>UPDATE:  </strong>[noon, Friday, May 19]  The Brooklyn College MFA Press Conference originally scheduled for 1 pm today has been POSTPONED.  Details to follow.</p>

<p><strong>FIGHTING THE CENSOR'S (ST)INK</strong></p>

<p>The location is the Law Offices of McLaughlin & Stern at 260 Madison Avenue (Betw. 38th and 39th) on the 20th Floor.  The time is 1 pm.</p>
