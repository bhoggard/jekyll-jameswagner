<p>If anyone was thinking of going to the <a href="https://jameswagner.com/index.php?m=200309#1062">A.R.T. Benefit</a>, and have not stopped by the gallery yet, I'd suggest getting there <i>as soon as you can</i>, that is, any time today.   The works are already being sold, but there's plenty of art left.</p>

<p>Prices range/d from $10 to $250, including some of their iris prints, although the most valuable are also available, and their prices top at $1250 - on works generally worth several times that.</p>

<p>The idea is to raise money which is absolutely needed by the institution, as well as to benefit young collectors and broadcast the artists at the same time.  I suspect nothing will remain after this evening, so it may be an especially interesting scene after 7.</p>

<p>We've already parted with some money ourselves, and we now learn so have some of our friends.  Yea for all of us!</p>
