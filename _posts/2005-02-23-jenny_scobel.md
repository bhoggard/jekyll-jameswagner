Jenny Scobel <em>Flutter in the Room</em> 2004 graphite, watercolor, oil and wax 32" x 24"</p>

<p>Friends already know I really like Jenny Scobel's work, but the current show at <a href="http://www.thomaserben.com/">Thomas Erben</a> has the best stuff I've seen yet.  These two may be my favorites.  I'll also admit that the backdrops are so exciting they make me shudder.</p>

<p>This is the first time we've seen a male image in her iconography, and the faces are usually anonymous.  Scobel's brother Quentin died of AIDS in 1996.</p>
