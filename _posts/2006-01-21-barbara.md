Niclaus Gerhaert von Leiden <em>reliquary bust of Saint Barbara</em> c. 1465 ashwood with polychromy [detail]</p>

<p>It's a totally weird <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saint_Barbara">story</a>, but her legendary beauty has inspired gorgeous work by artists for more than a millenium, including this one which grabbed my camera on my last visit to the Metropolitan Museum.</p>

<p>The attraction of the Catholic faithful to human beauty has ever been thus, and the current Roman pontiff appears to be <a href="http://p094.ezboard.com/fcatholicreformationfrm31.showMessage?topicID=34.topic">no exception</a>, judging from stories and pictures documenting the charm of his personal secretary.</p>
