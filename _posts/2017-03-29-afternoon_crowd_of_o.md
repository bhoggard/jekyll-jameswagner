<p>I was thinking of Weegee's '<a href="https://www.icp.org/browse/archive/objects/afternoon-crowd-at-coney-island-brooklyn-5">Afternoon Crowd at Coney Island, Brooklyn</a>', but the pansies were thinking of moving up and down Broadway.</p>

<p>This image, captured on Monday, recorded an ephemeral moment between the time these little pots were unloaded onto the street pavement and the time they were carried off to their separate planters to welcome spring.</p>
