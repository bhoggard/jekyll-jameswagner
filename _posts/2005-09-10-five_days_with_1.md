<p><strong>UPDATE:  "Five Days with Katrina" had moved and, thanks to Silvia Morales who told me where it went, the link below now works once again</strong></p>

<p>I haven't seen anything like this site before now.  This album, "<a href="http://www.kodakgallery.com/alvaromoralesimages">Five Days with Katrina</a>," is in the form of a five-day diary posted by someone who survived the New Orleans hurricane inside the French Quarter.  There are almost two hundred extraordiary images accompanied by some fascinating captions.</p>

<p>I haven't gone through more than a few dozen myself yet, but wanted to broadcast the site right away.  I have to say that the photographs of ancient deserted streets taken just before the storm hit are incredibly beautiful.</p>

<p>I don't know much about this wonderful witness, but his name is Alvaro R. Morales Villa.</p>
