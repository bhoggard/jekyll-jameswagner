Ryan Sarah Murphy <em>Untitled (Plots)</em> 2000 wood, 10,000+ clothespins, paint, gesso, glue, each component 34" x 21" x 6"</p>

<p><a href="http://www.outrageouslook.com/nowshowing.html">Ryan Sarah Murphy</a>'s installation was lying in the sun of the east window, just to the right of the door, when I walked into Outrageous Look last Sunday.  I checked out the beautiful <a href="/2007/04/gavin_green_at_1.html">Gavin Green</a> show in the gallery's main spaces, and went back to the window.  I was captivated.</p>

<p>It's now inside my head, as if I were still looking at it.  I can't really adequately account for this.  It's a simple pair of found wooden frames enclosing thousands of upright wooden clothespins, everything whitewashed.   I see more than one metaphor working here, but in the interest of your own visit I'll keep them to myself.</p>
