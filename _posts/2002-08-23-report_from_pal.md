<p>[Steve did report that his friend Erica had been arrested, and that she has support people here.  This report just arrived from <a href="http://www.jewsagainsttheoccupation.org/">JATO</a> (Jews Against The Occupation) rather than from Steve directly.]</p>

<blockquote>
<p>Nablus-A member of the New York-based group Jews Against the <br />
Occupation was arrested by the Israeli Military earlier today after <br />
delivering food and medicine to Palestinian families.   She has been<br />
falsely charged with shielding rock-throwing boys.</p>

<p>Erica Weitzman, a Manhattan-based humanitarian aid and human rights <br />
worker is currently being held at the Shogai Shomron military base <br />
and will be transferred to a prison at the Ariel settlement to await<br />
deportation.</p>

<p>Ms. Weitzman has been in the West Bank since August 9th, 2002 and has <br />
been visiting civilian Palestinian apartment buildings that have been <br />
taken over by the Israeli army for use as bases.  The army generally<br />
forces several families who live in the building into one small <br />
apartment.  Ms. Weitzman has been observing living conditions and <br />
bringing food and medicine to these families who are not permitted to <br />
leave their buildings, even during the short periods when curfew<br />
is lifted.  In the past 60 days in Nablus, the round-the-clock curfew <br />
has only been lifted for a total of 60 hours, most of which have been<br />
concentrated in time blocks.</p>

<p>Ms. Weitzman denies having contact with rock-throwing children.</p>

<p>"The more crimes the Israeli government commits against the <br />
Palestinian people in the name of Jews everywhere, the more I felt it <br />
was precisely my responsibility as a Jew to speak out against them."<br />
Ms. Weitzman said at a press conference held in front of the AIPAC <br />
offices in New York before leaving for the West Bank.</p>

<p>Members of Jews Against The Occupation have maintained a presence in <br />
the West Bank since April 2002, working in solidarity with non-<br />
violent Palestinian organizers against the daily military violence <br />
visited on Palestinian civilians. Jews Against The Occupation<br />
recognizes that the acts of the Israeli government are not only <br />
illegal and immoral, but pose a threat to Jewish lives and the fabric <br />
of Jewish identity worldwide.</p>
