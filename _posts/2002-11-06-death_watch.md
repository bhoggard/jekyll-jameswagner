<p>The junta in Washington frightened, seduced or merely distracted, us with a war of its own invention. Is this nation cowardly, insane or just plain stupid?</p>

<p>Never mind.  The consequences will be the same, regardless of the answer.</p>

<p>The people who illegitimately seized power in the last election now control every* institution of our national government and have free rein to accomplish their mean and dangerous program of greed and violence.</p>

<p>This was less an election than a second mugging.</p>

<p>The nation and the world are almost certainly doomed.  What a pity it has all been accomplished without a real contest, and by such small minds.</p>

<p>Ah, but you and I both now see that it's 5:30 in the morning!  Won't it look better in the daylight?</p>

<p>No.</p>

<p>* Think of all the federal judgeships in the land, and the nasty appointees on the way.<br />
