<p>[the following text is from Joe's press release]</p>

<p><b>Joe Ovelman</b></p>

<p>"Two Walls"</p>

<p>Appearing Saturday, September 13th</p>

<p>at the following locations:</p>

<p>10th Avenue, between 23rd and 24th Streets (Next to Car Wash)<br />
West 25th Street, between 10th and 11th Avenues (To the right of 531<br />
West 25th)</p>

<p>Joe Ovelman and Oliver Kamm/<a href="http://www.apartment5begallery.com/content.php">5BE Gallery</a> are pleased to announce the<br />
installation of two walls of images on Saturday, September 13. These<br />
are the fifth and sixth walls in Joe Ovelman's series of outside<br />
installations using walls from construction sites.</p>

<p>The walls consist of 124 feet of combined images culled from Ovelman's<br />
photographic work.</p>

<p>Joe Ovelman will have a solo show of new work at Oliver Kamm/5BE<br />
Gallery in February 2004.</p>
