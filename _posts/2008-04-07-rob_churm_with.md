<p>The Glasgow gallery <a href="http://www.sorchadallas.com/">Sorcha Dallas</a> displayed some terrific drawings by <a href="http://www.sorchadallas.com/exhibitions/820">Rob Churm</a> in their booth at Armory.  There was never any doubt some of them would show up on this blog.  The only question might have been which, but they were all so good I decided it didn't matter.</p>
