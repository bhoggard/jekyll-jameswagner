<p>Whew!  This <a href="/2008/01/some_ps1_shows_1.html">photo prohibition thingy</a> is becoming even more insane.  Check out Ed Winkleman's <a href="http://edwardwinkleman.blogspot.com/2008/01/feds-working-their-way-west-and.html">post</a> on the subject, and don't miss the comments and the several links you'll find there.</p>

<p>I apologize, to myself and to anyone else who might be concerned about the issue, that the current show at 303 Gallery is listed on ArtCal.</p>

<p>I have <a href="/2007/11/claire_oliver_s.html">written</a> on this site that neither I nor the calendar would include shows in galleries which do not permit photography.  The listing which appeared this month was simply an oversight.  It will stay up until the show closes, but only because <a href="http://jameskalmreport.blip.tv/#641098">James Kalm</a>'s provocative video is now <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/1/6134">tagged</a> on the ArtCal listing.  It's the last 303 show we will be listing until the gallery comes to its senses.</p>
