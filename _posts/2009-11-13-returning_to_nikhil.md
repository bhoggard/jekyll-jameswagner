<p>Barry and I returned to the New Museum on Sunday to witness the conclusion of <a href="/2009/11/nikhil_chopra_yog_ra.html">Nikhil Chopra</a>'s performance.   <a href="http://bloggy.com/2009/11/nikhil_chopra_new_museum.html">Bloggy</a> has the story, and more pictures.</p>

<p>We're both astounded that there hasn't been more coverage of this extraordinary piece.  The installation itself, including videos of earlier performances, remains in the lobby gallery through most of the winter.</p>

<p>We will be there again tonight at 7 for his <a href="http://www.newmuseum.org/events/380">lecture</a>.</p>
