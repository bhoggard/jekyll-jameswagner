<p>The overall design of the <a href="http://www.getty.edu/">Getty Center</a> is that of <a href="http://www.richardmeier.com/">Richard Meier</a>, but <a href="http://www.pacewildenstein.com/jsp/artists/RobertIrwin.jsp">Robert Irwin</a> was commissioned to create its Central Garden.  Both jobs cried out for gently-sloping walking ramps, but each man arrived at an elegant solution appropriate to his own creation.  Together they approach or surround a cultural temple atop a beautiful sun-drenched acropolis.</p>
