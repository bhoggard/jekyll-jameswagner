<a href="http://www.esterpartegas.com/">Ester Partegàs</a> <em>Yes Collection</em> 2007 resin, spray acrylic and enamel paint 12.25" x 82.75" x 59" [installation view]</p>

<p>Barcelona's <a href="http://www.noguerasblanchard.com/index.html">Nogueras Blanchard</a> gallery showed work by <a href="/2004/03/fun_and_yeah_ve.html">Ester Partegàs</a> at <a href="http://www.newartdealers.org/miami/2007/">NADA</a>, including this over-size identification bracelet.  I thought the gallery's images of a room installation, "Invaders", being put together at that moment in Madrid's <a href="http://www.museoreinasofia.es/s-exposiciones/exposicion.php?idexposicion=251">Reina Sofia</a> were very impressive, but light reflections made them impossible to record.</p>

<p>The museum page includes a statement about <a href="http://www.foxyproduction.com/artist/view/15">Partegàs</a>'s work in general which is even more useful than its description of <a href="http://en.www.mcu.es/novedades/novedadesEsterPartegas.html">the current installation</a> itself.   Wish I could see it.</p>
