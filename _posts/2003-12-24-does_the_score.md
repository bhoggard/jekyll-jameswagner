<p><b>Guerrillas Kill 3 U.S. Troops; Bomb Kills 5 Iraqis</b></p>

<p><br /><br />
This is the first Reuters headline I spotted on my home page as I got up this morning.  Before reading the <a href="http://story.news.yahoo.com/news?tmpl=story&cid=578&e=4&u=/nm/20031224/ts_nm/iraq_dc">story</a> I had perversely assumed that "we" were the party responsible for the deaths of the 5 Iraqis.  Actually it was the work of a suicide bomber in a northern Kurdish city, who died along with two guards, a passerby and a 13-year-old girl.</p>

<p>Now I would say that while the "score" should read, 8-0, it's actually the coach in the White House who deserves the credit for every one of those eight - and for every other death in this monstrous war that has been and is still to be.</p>
