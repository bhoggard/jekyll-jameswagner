Holly Coulis <em>Boy and Eagle</em> 2008 oil on linen 28" x 28"</p>

<p>I love this painting.  I can't say why, but I'm sure there's more to it than that it portrays a beautiful youth - not that I have anything against that, at least for a starter.  Holly Coulis's painting was just one work within an excellent-but-recently-closed group show, "<a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/6/7506">Admirer</a>", at 31 Grand, curated by the artist <a href="http://www.31grand.com/cavanaugh.html">Maureen Cavanaugh</a>.</p>
