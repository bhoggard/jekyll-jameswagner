<p>I'm tempted to describe it as heroic, but Paddy would laugh at me.   Art Fag City's <a href="http://www.artfagcity.com/2008/11/10/afc-receives-cease-and-desist-from-the-estate-of-helmut-newton/">post</a> brushing off frivolous claims of copyright infringement made by lawyers on behalf of <a href="http://www.taschen.com/pages/en/catalogue/photography/all/01354/facts.mrs_newton.htm">Alice Springs</a>, <a href="http://www.helmutnewton.com/">Helmut Newton</a>'s widow June, is spot-on.</p>

<p>And I'm <a href="/2008/01/more_re_photo_p_1.html">not unacquainted</a> with the discussion of photography and "fair use" myself, but AFC offers a full accounting of a real-life scenario, and help to all bloggers in the form of copies of documents and links, ending:</p>

<blockquote>
<p>Kowtowing to wrongfull copyright infringement claims is a dangerous precident I’m not willing to set.</p>
