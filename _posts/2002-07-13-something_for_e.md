<p>Great art for you, emergency medical relief for a beleagured Palestinian population, and a little recognition for some brilliant and courageous artists and the people who organized an extraordinary event at <a href="http://www.whiteboxny.org/">White Box </a>gallery over the last several weeks:</p>

<p>The "Come to <b>Life</b>" <a href="http://www.thing.net/~cometolife/">benefit auction </a>ended last evening, and we were fortunate to be able to help a woefully undersolicited charity at the same time we were able to take home a fantastic art thing, but there are still great pieces in all media available for purchase.</p>

<p>The reason that not everything was snatched up last night is simply the fact that there was virtually no publicity.  The works were all donated by the artists (the list should accordingly be considered a roll of honor), and only one curator, but not one gallery and not one representative of the media had both the desire and the courage to be connected with the event in any way.  Some had expressed sympathy or support privately but confessed they could not afford to announce the fact or do anything which might connect them to the benefit, because of fear of repercussions.</p>

<p>This is appalling, but I suppose it should not have been a surprise.</p>

<p>What, are the Palestinians all lepers now?</p>

<p>If you have anything of an art budget and any interest in bold new work,<br />
you can affirm your own commitment to justice at the same time by running to the phone as soon as you can and calling Noritoshi Hirakawa, who is handling the remains of the event, at (718) 302-4199, or talk to me for more background.</p>
