<p>When we walked into the show at <a href="http://www.feigencontemporary.com/">Feigen Contgemporary</a> on Saturday afternoon the fantastic art of a brilliant outsider, Eugene Von Bruenchenhein, was totally new to us, but not to many others, as we quickly discovered.</p>

<p>The exhibition closed that day, but for anyone seduced by the image above, beyond what is available on the gallery website there are many more in a number of media (and a fascinating story) easily Googled.</p>

<p>His wife was the model in his photograpy, and his muse for decades, but she wasn't the only one who was hot:</p>
