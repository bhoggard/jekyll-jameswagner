<p>Some of our friends seem quite happy with the kind of weather that makes me pretty miserable.  I snapped these happy guys on 10th Avenue in front of the Red Cat late Wednesday afternoon.   It had rained lightly all day, and although it wasn't as warm as it is today, the humidity must have hovered around 100% when I passed them at six o'clock.</p>

<p>For the floriculturally challenged, the common names for these blossoms are, from the top, <a href="/2006/06/barry.html">Impatiens</a>, <a href="/2004/06/loving_the_hibi.html">Hibiscus</a> and <a href="/2006/10/morning_glory_t.html">Morning Glory</a>.</p>
