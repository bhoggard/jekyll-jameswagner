<p><img alt="NYstandsguard.jpg" src="/mt_archives/NYstandsguard.jpg" width="280" height="210" border="0" /></p>

<p>This is the image which accompanies the <a href="http://www.cnn.com/2004/ALLPOLITICS/08/27/gop.security/index.html">lead story</a> on the CNN site at this moment.  The headline on the front page?  "New York stands guard."  I see it as, "Republican Guards hold up New York."  We are an occupied city tonight, as I listen to the sirens wailing up and down the avenues and helicopters scanning with high-powered searchlights as they whop, whop, whop overhead.</p>

<p>I'm punching this post into my blog as we're listening to a remarkable webstream reporting tonight's ongoing, historical <a href="http://www.times-up.org/cm.php">Critical Mass</a> through the streets of Manhattan, including live accounts by the cyclist participants.  There are reports that scores of people have been arrested already - for bicycling while smiling.  The police are funnelling them into police vans and buses, chasing away the public and prohibiting photographs.</p>

<p>In the end, 50,000 cops in one city are going to find something to keep them amused.</p>

<p>Bloomberg and Kelly are doing Bush's work.  Nothing could be more effective in radicalizing and provoking orderly protests than this outrageous over-reaction.</p>

<p><b>UPDATE:</b>  The <a href="http://www.washingtonpost.com/ac2/wp-dyn/A40504-2004Aug27?language=printer"><u>Washington</u> <u>Post</u></a> reports that nearly 250 bicyclists were arrested.  This is insane!</p>
