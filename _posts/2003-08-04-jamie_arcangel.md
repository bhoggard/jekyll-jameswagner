<p>I don't think I could begin to do justice to yesterday's events at the courageous <a href="http://www.teamgal.com/">Team Gallery</a> with any written description, so I'm going to cop out altogether and leave it to a few pictures, which at least have the value of their uniqueness.  If they're a bit pretty, it's a very good, and an appropriate-to-the-subject thing.  What they can't do is register the energy in the dark Team rooms last night.</p>

<p>Actually we were there for little more than the set of Jamie Arcangel and the Arcangels, and their earlier incarnation, Insectiside, so I couldn't speak to the remainder of the schedule in any event.</p>

<p>The "Throwback" show installations in the gallery, by <a href="http://www.beigerecords.com/cory/">Cory Arcangel & Beige</a>, <a href="http://www.teamgal.com/marshall/index02.html">Maria Marshall</a>, <a href="http://www.teamgal.com/routson/new.html">Jon Routson</a>, and the almost-free take-homes (genuine art as tees, music, posters, prints, books), were exciting, seductive and really, really beautiful.  The music was dynamite ("nuclear" just doesn't seem right these days)!</p>

<p>For more on Cory himself, see <a href="http://www.digitalmediatree.com/tommoody/?19538">Tom Moody</a>.</p>
