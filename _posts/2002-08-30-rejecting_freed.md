<p>Alright, I'm back.  I've recovered just a little from the impact of the item I posted below.</p>

<p>Still I can make only a very few observations for now.  Just how much free speech is "too much" free speech?</p>

<p>Even more to the point, whose speech will say my speech is <i>too</i> free speech?</p>

<p>And finally, this is a citizenry which has decided that the huge corporate payoffs which determine the choice of all of our presidents, legislators, governors and even the composition of our courts, supreme or otherwise, is and must remain free speech, yet half of those same citizens think there is too much freedom of <i>real</i> speech, meaning yours and mine and also that of the press from whom they get all of their information.</p>

<p>How do people like this get through even an ordinary day?</p>
