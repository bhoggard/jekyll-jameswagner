<p>The pizza was delivered this eveing by a tall, strong, young man with a distinct German accent.  Young German delivering pizza in Manhattan in 2002?  Shouldn't he be on Wall Street or partying on the Upper East Side?We don't know what his job means, to him or to us, but we wish him well, as we do all of the wonderful guys who work so hard peddling through the city feeding its people.  Their kids will go to Columbia, or at least they will have a chance to do so if they want to.  So we hope and wish.</p>

<p>Yea New York!  <i>Es lebe</i> the pizza guys!</p>
