<p>Last night's opening at <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/7/300">Deitch</a> was an almost ecstatically-happy sorta-rave made up mostly of artists and computer nerds.  The output of this little machine, an altered vintage Nintendo cartridge, sitting on a black platform in the corner of the room, was what attracted the overflow crowd.  The machine itself was the product of a particulary felicitous collaboration between <a href="/2004/04/paper_rad.html">Paper Rad</a> and <a href="/2005/01/cory_archangel_1.html">Cory Arcangel</a>.</p>

<p><a href="http://www.digitalmediatree.com/tommoody/?30722">Tom Moody</a> describes the work - in two posts.</p>
