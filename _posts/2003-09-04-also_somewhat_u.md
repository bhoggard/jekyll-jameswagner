Illustration by David Olére, a <i>Sonderkommando</i> who survived nearly two years at <a href="http://www.auschwitz-muzeum.oswiecim.pl/html/eng/start/index.php">Auschwitz</a></p>

<p>Anybody feel funny about this <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2003/09/04/international/europe/04AUSC.html?pagewanted=print&position=">announcement</a>?</p>

<blockquote>
<p>The Israeli Embassy in Warsaw said three Israeli jets piloted by descendants of Holocaust survivors would fly over the former [Auschwitz death] camp at noon on Thursday. They were to be joined by two Polish MIG-29 jets.</p>
</blockquote>

<p>Well, the authorities of the museum there do.</p>

<blockquote>
<p>"It's a cemetery, a place of silence and concentration," a museum spokesman, Jaroslaw Mensfelt, said. He called the planned flyover "a demonstration of military might which is an entirely inappropriate way to commemorate the victims."</p>
</blockquote>
