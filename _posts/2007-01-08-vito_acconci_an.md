<p>D'Amelio Terras opened two very interesting shows on Saturday, a <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/1/3708">group show</a> of works by Vito Acconci, John M Armleder, Olivier Mosset, Chuck Nanney, Steven Parrino and Sam Samore with the intriguing title, "The loss of history makes them constantly curious and continuously horny....", and an exquisite exhibition of <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/1/3709">Dike Blair</a> paintings.</p>

<p>Even without the help of the 1995 structure whose parameters and inspiration is outlined in the gallery's press release the work in the larger space looks absolutely stunning.</p>

<p>In the smaller room at the front of the gallery there are some beautiful small gouache-and-pencil works by Blair from the late 80's and early 90's.</p>
