<p>Today the <u>NYTimes</u> provided a <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2002/06/09/weekinreview/09BWAR.html">follow-up </a>to the item I posted on June 3 about the Masai gift to the people of the United States.  In spite of earlier reports, the cows may be on their way here after all.</p>

<blockquote>
<p>"If we can get 80,000 men and machines into Afghanistan, we can get 14 cows out of Kenya," [a <u>Washington</u> <u>Times</u> columnist] wrote. Others, too, want the cows to come home. One woman suggested they go to the Bronx Zoo. Another imagined putting them out to pasture — in Central Park.</p>
</blockquote>
