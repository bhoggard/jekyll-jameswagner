<p>Sorry, but I forgot to ask for specifics about the sculpture, since we were virtually closing the restaurant Wednesday night when I snapped this image and there was no one around at the time who might have been helpful.</p>

<p>We were leaving our new neigborhood "nouvelle" Indian restaurant, <a href="http://www.sepiamutiny.com/sepia/archives/001035.html">Bombay Talkie</a>.  This had been our third visit, a late supper with a friend following the new David Mamet play at the <a href="http://www.atlantictheater.com/">Atlantic Theater Company</a>.  Our little party gave mixed reviews for both the restaurant and the play, but in Chelsea, which sadly does not have a single really decent restaurant (okay, maybe one), the fact that the run of this convenient and at least slightly diverting eatery will be longer than the somewhat baffling "<a href="http://theater2.nytimes.com/2005/03/02/theater/reviews/02roma.html">Romance</a>" means that we will probably be back.</p>
