<a href="/2006/01/the_group_at_ol.html">Tom Meacham</a> <em>Brian Blade is a Better Kisser Than Me</em> 2006 acrylic on canvas 90" x 60" [installation view]</p>

<p>Every single work in this delicious at Oliver Kamm's <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/1/2317">5BE show</a> can be appreciated for its thing-ness as much as its painting-ness.  Looking doesn't seem to be enough, but I did manage to keep to my side the hand which was not occupied with the camera.</p>

<p>I don't understand how all the physical stuff came about, unless it had something to do with the particular conceit of the show:  Two gallery artists were each asked to choose another artist who would be included in an intimate show of four.  <a href="http://www.5begallery.com/artist/view/245">Tom Meacham</a> chose <a href="http://www.cultureport.com/cultureport/artists/stephan/">Gary Stephan</a> and <a href="http://www.5begallery.com/artist/view/233">Cheryl Donegan</a> selected <a href="http://query.nytimes.com/gst/fullpage.html?res=9505E3DA1E3AF937A35754C0A9659C8B63">Stephanie Campos</a>.</p>

<p>Everything works.  The art is all very fine and the installation is perfect.</p>
