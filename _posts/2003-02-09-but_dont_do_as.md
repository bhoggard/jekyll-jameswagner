<p>Let us get this straight.  The Bushies are going into Iraq to prevent that country from using weapons of mass destruction, including nuclear devices and chemical agents.  But they have <a href="http://www.knoxnews.com/kns/world/article/0,1406,KNS_351_1727182,00.html">already said</a> that to do so they may use nuclear devices themselves, and now they <a href="http://www.nti.org/d_newswire/issues/newswires/2003_2_6.html#11">are saying</a> they may also use chemical agents.</p>

<p>All this is supposed to make sense and appeal to the conscience of the world?</p>
