<p>I just took another look at the <a href="http://www.biddingforgood.com/auction/AuctionHome.action?auctionId=103271077">BAMart Silent Auction</a> site today and I ended up excited on two accounts:  for BAM's early success (many of the works are already above their estimates) and for the terrific opportunities available to patrons and visual art lovers who may have a little extra cash right now.</p>

<p>I've uploaded images of some of the works here, but there are some 150+ others on the <a href="http://www.biddingforgood.com/auction/AuctionHome.action?auctionId=103271077">BAMart site</a>.</p>

<p>It's not necessary to go anywhere to be a part of the event;  the dedicated site shows all the works, describes the details, and makes it easy to bid.</p>

<p>The bidding ends at 8 this evening.</p>
