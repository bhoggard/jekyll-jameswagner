<p><a href="/2005/06/ara_peterson_an.html">Ara Peterson</a> is included in a group show at <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/1/934">Greene Naftali</a> based loosely on the idea of fractal geometry.  Both the concept and its execution suggest the mystical as much as the scientific.  A stunning show, and there are some great images on the gallery site itself.</p>

<p>In addition to Peterson's work there are fascinating contributions from Julie Becker, Keith Connolly, David Dempewolf, Rachel Harrison and Michaela Meise.</p>
