<p>First, I have to apologize for the highlights at the top of this image, but when you're perched inside an historic armory dedicated to a noble citizen militia neither a gallery nor a photographer can entirely control of the ambient lighting.  Fortunately the beauty of this large painting by Chris Duncan is evident even with lighting that is less than ideal.</p>

<p><a href="http://www.baileygallery.com/">Jeff Bailey</a> showed this grand painting at <a href="http://www.pulse-art.com/ny/">Pulse</a>.  There are a many more images of the artist's work on the gallery's site.</p>
