<p>I spotted these two <a href="http://www.smartusa.com/">Smart</a>s on the same block of West 21st Street this past Tuesday.  They were both parked on the north side of the street, the front end of each safely (although barely) behind lines across which they legally cannot venture, back bumpers only a hair away from much larger vehicles parked to the rear.</p>

<p>These shots show that <a href="/2007/12/smart_on_23rd_s_1.html">here</a> in New York, and in Europe for ten years now, size really does matter.</p>
