<p>Dunno exactly why, but <a href="http://www.tinjail.com/drinkAndDraw/">this</a> sounds like a wonderful thing.</p>

<p>The promoters (yeah, that sounds so big-deal), <a href="http://www.mteww.com/">M.River and T.Whid</a>, have <em>their</em> explanation:</p>

<blockquote>
<p>It might be interesting if an art idea conceived in a bar could use a bar as a site and context for said art idea and it's been a long hard winter.</p>
</blockquote>

<p>But I like the sense of place and proportion provided by the description of the first prize:</p>

<blockquote>
<p>Win a $100 bar tab [at the event's venue, Greenpoint's Bar Matchless]</p>
</blockquote>

<p>This year <a href="http://www.inka-essenhigh.com/">Inka Essenhigh</a> and <a href="http://www.artnet.com/Magazine/features/baghdadjournal.asp">Steve Mumford</a> will be the judges.</p>

<p>For images from last year's event, go to <a href="http://www.twhid.com/photos/drinkinDrawin/index.html">MTAA</a>.</p>
