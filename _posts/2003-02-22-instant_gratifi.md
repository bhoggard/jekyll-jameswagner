<p>Musicals bore me, but I love <a href="http://www.haring.com/">Keith Haring</a>.</p>

<p>I also remember the <a href="http://www.disco-disco.com/clubs/paradise.html">Paradise Garage</a>.</p>

<p>So I went to the spanking new musical, "Radiant Baby," last night at <a href="http://www.publictheater.org/">the Public Theater</a>.  Second row.</p>

<p>One of the characters in the production yells (I think twice) "Paradise is closed!"  Well, it re-opened last night for me.  It probably re-opened for everyone in the audience, even if they had never been there or even never heard of it, and it wasn't just the over-the-top production number, "Paradise/Instant Gratification," that did it.</p>

<p>The whole thing isn't perfect, but the cast is!</p>

<p>Does it mean anything that I couldn't let my handkerchief back in my pocket during the entire second act?</p>

<p>Don't miss it.  Instant gratification.</p>
