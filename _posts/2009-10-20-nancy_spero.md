<p>Nancy Spero <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2009/10/20/arts/design/20spero.html?_r=1&ref=obituaries">died on Sunday</a>.   We miss her already.   But while the world is made so much poorer with our loss, her great spirit and her phenomenal work will always brighten our lives - and strengthen our resolve to be almost worthy of her courage.</p>
