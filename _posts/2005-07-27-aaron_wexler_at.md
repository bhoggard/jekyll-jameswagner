<p>- being another work from the <a href="http://bloggy.com/mt/archives/004990.html">current show</a> at Jack the Pelican Presents.  Barry and I had <a href="http://bloggy.com/mt/archives/004216.html">first</a> seen <a href="http://www.aaronwexler.com/">Aaron Wexler</a>'s beautiful art exactly one year ago at Oliver Kamm's 5BE Gallery in a <a href="http://www.5begallery.com/content.php?mode=gallery&section_id=32">show</a> curated by Lital Mehr.  Wexler's older paper collages were so subtle they were virtually invisible, especially in photo reproduction.  I only had to wait.</p>
