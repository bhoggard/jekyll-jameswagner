<p>The Great Wall of China, Hadrian's wall, the Berlin Wall and the Apartheid Wall in the West Bank were and are all monuments to the failure of societies to relate to <em>the other</em>.  Our own wall of exclusion along borders we captured from Mexico by force is an equally crude weapon and, like all the others, it's intended to separate people who are otherwise essentially indivisible.  Some day we will understand this, but at the moment our ignorance and our fears are being successfully manipulated by demagogues.</p>
