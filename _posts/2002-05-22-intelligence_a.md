<p>Reuters <a href="http://www.reuters.com/news_article.jhtml;jsessionid=1YD4NCBA0QWSSCRBAEZSFEYKEEATIIWD?type=topnews&StoryID=996985">reports</a> that Cheney is worried about people finding out stuff.</p>

<blockquote>
<p>WASHINGTON (Reuters) - Vice President Dick Cheney said on Wednesday he feared a new Sept. 11 inquiry proposed by the top Senate Democrat would leak intelligence secrets and undercut U.S. efforts to prevent future attacks.</p>
</blockquote>

<p>Aside from the more than arguable theoretical merits of his position, it seems to me the security problem up to now has been that government "intelligence" has been entirely <i>too</i> secret for our own good.</p>
