<p>We can never have too much park - or too many artists - and an "artist's park" sounds like a good thing to me, even if it's smaller than a nightstand.   I came upon this piece by <a href="http://www.mahangallery.com/artists/scot_kaplan.php">Scot Kaplan</a> on the south side of West 24 Street while bouncing from one gallery to the next two days ago.   Maybe it's also some kind of homage to the Beuys <a href="/2005/03/bueys_is_deadli.html">basalt pieces</a> two blocks south on 22 Street.   The sod looks like it just got there, but I did spot a small piece of cellophane and one bird feather lying on the grass.</p>

<p>The gallery<em>ese</em> verbiage in the caption below the photo is mostly my invention.</p>

<p>I really hope this thing's a permanent installation.</p>
