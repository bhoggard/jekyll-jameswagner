<p>The <a href="http://www.bluebutton.info">Blue Button Project</a> is beginning to take on a life of its own.</p>

<p>I've watched it as it evolved out of frustration and horror into a resolve and finally a tool with which to do something about the terror U.S. policy is responsible for around the world.   I'm exhilarated to be now regularly running into people on the streets and elsewhere who are wearing the Blue Button and willing to express resistance to the injuries being done to all of us around the world, supposedly at our own bidding and for our own good.</p>

<p>Check out the site now, and see how or where you can find the Button, and please let us know if you have any ideas for increasing its visibility.</p>
