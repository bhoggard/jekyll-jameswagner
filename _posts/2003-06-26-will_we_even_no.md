<p>Today, Thursday, on what is essentially the beginning of "Gay Pride" weekend, the Supremes will <a href="http://www.cnn.com/2003/LAW/06/25/scotus.sodomy/">announce</a> their pontifical decision about whether us queers may have sex.</p>

<p>Either they're going to be delivering good news, or they're really asking for trouble.  If it's the latter, will there be rioting in the streets, or will we be too busy partying in our ghettos to notice or care?</p>

<p>Let's <a href="http://www.datalounge.com/datalounge/news/record.html?record=20802">be ready</a> across the country, either way.  In New York, the designated time and site is 7 in the evening, Sheridan Square (7th Avenue/Christopher Street).</p>
