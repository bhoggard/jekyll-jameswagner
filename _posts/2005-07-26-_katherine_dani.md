Katherine Daniels <em>Multi-Colored Pendant</em> 2003 wired plastic beads on plastic spool [large detail of installation, with details of two works on paper in the background]</p>

<p>I realize this site has been looking pretty grim lately, and that I was risking the loss of its art blog aspect, so I went through my photo stash and found something which would brighten up the space and at the same time show some beautiful work most people would not have seen yet.</p>

<p>This piece by <a href="http://www.katherinedaniels.com/">Katherine Daniels</a> was installed in one of the rooms at the <a href="http://www.poolartfair.com/">Pool Art Fair</a> earlier this month.  Daniels was included in the show <a href="http://www.galleryartist.com/davidgibson/index.php?artist=129&ptr=resume">David Gibson</a> curated at Jack the Pelican last April, "<a href="/2005/04/culture_vulture.html">Culture Vulture</a>," with another wonderful, even more extravagant piece.</p>
