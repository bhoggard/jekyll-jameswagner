<p>The 2003 Ford ITU New York City <a href="http://www.triathlon.org/world-cup/wcup2003/new-york/index.htm">World Cup</a> Duathlon [sic], a "dry-tri," because of the polluting effect of <strike>recent</strike> perpetual heavy rainfall in Manhattan, was staged completely within Central Park on Sunday, incidently making it easier on the spectator, and especially the spectator's eye.  Who's designing the <a href="http://www.triathlon.org/world-cup/wcup2003/new-york/nyc-gallery/pages/nyc03-men-05_jpg.htm">boys'</a> and <a href="http://www.triathlon.org/world-cup/wcup2003/new-york/nyc-gallery/pages/nyc03-women-10_jpg.htm">girls'</a> costumes?</p>
