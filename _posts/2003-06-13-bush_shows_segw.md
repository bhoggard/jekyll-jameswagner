<p>The Segway is <a href="http://theitquestion.com/">supposed</a> to be foolproof - "A two-wheeled, intuitive personal transportation device that won't fall.  This super-smart, computer chip-laden machine won't topple with a driver's clumsiness."</p>

<p>This week our very own <i>chief</i> fool showed this to be just plain wrong.</p>

<blockquote>
<p>US President George Bush has been photographed falling off a high-tech scooter near his family's summer home in Kennebunkport, Maine.</p>

<p>A <a href="http://www.itv.com/news/864237.html">sequence of photos</a> show President Bush stepping onto a two-wheeled high-tech scooter and then lurching forward before recovering his balance.</p>
</blockquote>
