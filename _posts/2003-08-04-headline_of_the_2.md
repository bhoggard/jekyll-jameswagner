<p>"<b>J. Lo and Affleck Finally Get Some Privacy</b>," is the headline the <u>NYTimes</u> uses in an illustrated business (media) section <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2003/08/04/business/media/04GIGL.html">article</a> today.</p>

<blockquote>
<p>The story of Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck, glamorous movie stars whose love affair blossomed on a Hollywood set, has turned into the summer's most watched romance. The movie they made, however, has had no such luck.</p>
</blockquote>

<p>The film is a disaster.  The Times piece speculates on the overexposure factor.
