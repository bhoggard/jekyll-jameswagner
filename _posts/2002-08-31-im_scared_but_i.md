<p>A pretty  real piece, "<b>Victims of Realtime</b>...on the bizarre memorialization of 911," currently appears on the first page of the refreshingly-irreverent site, <a href="http://www.holyfuckingshitday.com/">HOLY FUCKING SHIT DAY</a>.</p>

<p>I posted my own comment there, but I'm including it below as well, to bring more forward my feelings about what's planned for next week in New York and elsewhere in the country.  We're out of here.</p>

<blockquote>
<p>We'll be in Europe most of September, where I expect to appreciate people acting like grown-ups about this thing, allowing us to do the same.</p>

<p>Just serendipity. We had planned the trip last spring, without even thinking about what we would be missing at home here in New York. It just hadn't occurred to me that the anniversary would end up looking like such a monster.</p>
</blockquote>

<p>What is being planned is not a memorial.  It's an obsessive orgy of victimhood.  I know of history, and it is my passion.  It is not history we are recording, but a self-serving and dangerous myth which will forever conceal the truth which we absolutely need in order to move forward.</p>
