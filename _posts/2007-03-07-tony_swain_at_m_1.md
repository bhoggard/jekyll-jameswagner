Tony Swain <em>Stuck Cuts</em> 2006 acrylic on newpaper  26.5" x 25.25" [installation view]</p>

<p>Glasgow's <a href="http://www.themoderninstitute.com/">The Modern Institute</a> showed this handsome drawing by the artist and musician <a href="http://www.scotlandandvenicebiennale.com/artists-tony-swain">Tony Swain</a> (<a href="http://profile.myspace.com/index.cfm?fuseaction=user.viewprofile&friendID=136079766">Hassle Hound</a>) in their booth at the <a href="http://www.thearmoryshow.com/">Armory</a>.</p>

<p>Swain is one of the six artists chosen to represent Scotland at the Venice Biennale this year.</p>
