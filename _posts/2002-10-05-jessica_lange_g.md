<p>--not that the media is going to report her <a href="http://www.iol.co.za/index.php?click_id=22&art_id=ct20020926221537276J000505">statement</a>, even if she is a celebrity (shouldn't that be enough of a temptation?), when it refuses to cover the statements of (legitimate?) politicians who speak out against the Administration's latest <i>divertissement</i>.</p>

<p>In a press conference at the San Sebastian film festival in Spain, where she received a lifetime achievement award, <a href="http://us.imdb.com/Name?Lange,+Jessica">Lange</a> did not mince words.</p>

<blockquote>
<p>"Bush stole the elections and since then we have all been suffering the consequences. The Iraq plan is absolutely mad, but what I do not understand is that nobody tries to stop it - neither inside nor outside the United States," the actress added.</p>

<p>"The atmosphere in my country is poisonous, intolerable for those of us who are not right-wing, so thank you for inviting me to this festival and allowing me to get out for a few days," Lange said on receiving the <a href="http://www.sansebastianfestival.ya.com/2002/in50/donostia.htm#1">Donostia Prize</a>, presented by Spanish actor Jose Coronado.</p>
</blockquote>

<p>Thanks, Jessica, for trying to stir up the waters a bit.</p>
