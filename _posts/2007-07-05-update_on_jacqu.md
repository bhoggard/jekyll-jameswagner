<p>On Tuesday I <a href="/2007/07/jacques_louis_v.html">wrote</a> that Jacques Louis Vidal had scheduled performances through two more weekends during his current show at <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/6/4980">Sunday</a> on the Lower East Side [LES], but today I've received word that he has reduced them to a single day's production, to be mounted this Saturday afternoon, July 7.</p>

<p>It will be a workshop at the gallery called "making friends + keeping friends", and should start soon after the mesquite cookout, which is on the schedule from 1 to 4.  I definitely recommend a visit to Eldridge Street on Saturday.</p>
