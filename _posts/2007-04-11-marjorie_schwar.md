<p>Meanwhile, demonstrating that no one should depend on this site for sufficient notice even of stuff I really like, I'm only now uploading a few images from a double <a href="http://www.stirusfreeus.com/schwarz_schwarz.html">show</a> which closed three days ago at Clayton Sean Horton's SUNDAY gallery.</p>

<p>The paintings in the main space were by Ludwig Schwarz.  Marjorie Schwarz showed drawings and collages in the project room.</p>

<p>SUNDAY is a neat and very welcome little space in the Lower East Side on Eldridge Street and I was excited about it even before learning that <a href="/2006/03/_well_be_at_the.html">Jacques Louis Vidal</a> has been asked to fill the main room in a show opening June 21.  Vidal was in the <a href="http://danglingbetween.com/">show</a> Barry and I curated last fall at Dam, Stuhltrager.</p>
