<p>The first show of the fall season at Eleven Rivington is curated by Fernanda Arruda.  "<a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/6/7756">Active Forms</a>" includes work by three young South American artists currently working in São Paolo.  The sculptures and works on paper by Edgard de Souza, Marcius Galan and Camila Sposati are displayed here, literally, in the shadow of several small hanging drawings by the Swiss-born Brazilian artist <a href="http://www.moma.org/exhibitions/exhibitions.php?id=9519&ref=calendar">Mira Schendel</a> (1919-1988), a pioneer in conceptual abstraction most familiar for work which combined language and paper.</p>
