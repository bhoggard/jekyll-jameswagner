Bruce Conner <em>Untitled</em> 1960 mixed media, pearls, nylon, mesh, wire, etc.  20" x 24.5" x 2.5"</p>

<p>I included this image in the <a href="/2005/03/bruce_conner_at.html">first post</a> I did about work I'd seen at the 2005 Armory Show.  I remember how excited I was at the time, particularly as I had known nothing (or <em>thought</em> I knew nothing, since I had actually experienced his art for years) about <a href="http://www.gallerypauleanglim.com/Gallery_Paule_Anglim/Bruce_COnner.html">Bruce</a> <a href="http://www.kohngallery.com/conner/artist_page.html">Connor</a> before I came across this piece, and he certainly wasn't newly-arrived on the scene.  My excitement continues, sadly mixed with the <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2008/07/09/arts/design/09conner.html?ref=obituaries">news</a> that Connor died on Monday at his home in San Francisco.</p>
