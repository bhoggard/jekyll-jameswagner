<p>Both towers of the Brooklyn Bridge are reflected in the waters of the East [er, . . . <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/East_river"><em>tidal strait</em>]</a> at the top of this photograph.  The picture was taken yesterday from the deck outside the <a href="/2006/08/darmstadt_and_j.html">Spiegeltent</a> on Pier 17.  Later in the evening those outdoor spaces were the site of the presentation of <a href="http://www.danspaceproject.org/programs/bessies.html">The Bessies</a>.</p>

<p>And, yes, the water you see here was moving north at a good clip.</p>
