<p>Some of you may already be tired of seeing this image, but as I'm one of a very select number given the responsibility for publicizing this show please humor me.  Genuine pictures of the installation will have to wait until the installing has been done.</p>

<p>"<a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/2/3175">Dangling Between The Real Thing And The Sign In The Window</a>" finally opens on Friday  [see the link to the left for the press release].   I expect so many gazillions of creative, smart, cute people to head for <a href="http://www.artcal.net/location/view/2/270">Dam, Stuhltrager</a> that night that Barry and I will have to retreat to the garden for some air, where Susan Dessel's <a href="/2006/09/yard.html">installation</a> will remain through the run of the show inside.   <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/2/3224">Loren Munk</a>'s work will be installed in the front room during the same month.  If you can't make it on Friday, please stop in later in the weekend, or on any of the next four.  The show closes on the 13th of November.  After the opening reception the gallery will be open on Fridays from 3 until 8 and Saturdays and Sundays from noon until 6.  You can also make an appointment.</p>

<p>Come and say hi.  We're going to try to amaze you.</p>
