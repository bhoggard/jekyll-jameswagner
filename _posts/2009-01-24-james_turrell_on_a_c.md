<p>It was cloudless, yes, but just a mite hazy - and freezing cold.  Still, sitting inside that quiet, perfect <a href="http://ps1.org/exhibitions/view/170">room</a> at PS1 (quiet once the voluble little kids had left) while it was still light outside, and looking up through the open roof was, . . .  ineffable.</p>
