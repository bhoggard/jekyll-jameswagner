<p>And those who live in New York City are the smartest of all.</p>

<p>Recent Gallup and Siena <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2003/02/21/nyregion/21POLL.html">polls</a>, when compared, show that the prospect of bombing and invading Iraq is less popular in New York State than in the nation as a whole, and still less popular in the city whose losses of September 11 are the White House's excuse for the war.</p>

<p>The concept and call, <a href="http://www.notinourname.net/">"not in our name"</a> started in New York and it's support still remains strongest in this most American of places.</p>
