Rashid Johnson <em>Run</em> 2008 mirror with spray paint 53" x 65" (including frame) [installation view]</p>

<p>Almost the first piece the visitor sees is a large, five foot mirror covered with the paint-sprayed word, "RUN".  Perhaps a tough auspice for an artist's first New York solo appearance, but it definitely shouldn't be taken as an exhortation.  Rashid Johnson's installation at Nicole Klagsbrun, "<a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/1/6490">The Dead Lecturer</a>", is one very fine show, its photographs and sculptures culled from, as the press release tells us:"a mythic secret society of African-American intelligentsia within a metaphysical landscape removed from time and history."</p>

<p>There are some very good images on the gallery's <a href="http://www.nicoleklagsbrun.com/exhibitions.html">site</a>, but a visit and the naked eye would do much better.</p>
