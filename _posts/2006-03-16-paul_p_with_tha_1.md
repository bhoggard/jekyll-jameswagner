<p>Paul P. was represented during last week's fairs by at least two galleries on nearly opposite sides of the earth.  The image above, shown here with a pop-up detail, appeared at the Armory show in the booth of <a href="http://www.ropac.net/">Galerie Thaddeus Ropac</a> (Paris and Salzburg), the two paintings below were shown at LA ART by <a href="http://www.marcselwynfineart.com/">Marc Selwyn Fine Art</a> (Los Angeles).</p>
