<p>It's only a coincidence that this image and post follows my lizard report, but I like the connection.  <a href="http://greenmuseum.org/artist_index.php?artist_id=19">Brandon Ballengée</a>'s skate was one of the works in a very interesting show at <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/13/903">Archibald Arts</a> we <a href="http://bloggy.com/mt/archives/005002.html">visited</a> on Saturday.  Ballengée is an environmental artist fascinated by fish and amphibians, notably lizards.  In a remarkable show of haunting images of nature, and our corruption of nature,  "Cleared and Stained Clearnose Skate" was the beautiful elephant in the middle of the room.</p>
