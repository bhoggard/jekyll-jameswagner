<p>A very subjective and definitely only partial list of some of the good stuff in Williamsburg galleries this week:</p>

<p><a href="http://www.momentaart.org/cur_pro/thai_art.html">Manit Sriwanichpoom</a>'s ghostly pink photographic provocations at Momenta<br />
72 Berry</p>

<p><a href="http://www.space1026.com/people/artists/ajw/bio.html">Andrew Jeffrey Wright</a>'s delightful and very smart conscience drawings at Champion<br />
281 N. 7th</p>

<p><a href="http://www.jessicamurrayprojects.com/artistsJackieGendel.html">Jackie Gendel</a>'s gorgeous waxy oils at Jessica Murray, esp, <em>Kablasto</em>!<br />
210 N. 6th</p>

<p><a href="http://www.blackandwhiteartgallery.com/gale/">Meighan Gale</a>'s breathtakingly intimate effortlessly majestic self-portraits at Black & White<br />
483 Driggs</p>

<p><a href="http://www.bellwethergallery.com/hall.html">Everest Hall</a>'s shameless sourced brush and pencil images at Bellwether<br />
335 Grand</p>

<p><a href="http://www.andrealoefke.com/text_pages/home.html">Andrea Loefke</a>'s enigmatic sculptures and tiny drawing constructions at S1<br />
242 S. 1st</p>

<p><a href="http://www.timestereo.com/pix.php?nice_day=24">Beatriz Monteavaro</a>'s beautiful Picasso/Planet of the Apes obsessions at Monya Rowe<br />
242 S. 1st</p>

<p><a href="http://www.plusultragallery.com/artists.html">Joe Fig</a>'s sculptural reconfigurations of painters' studios at Plus Ultra<br />
235 S. 1st</p>

<p><a href="http://www.brooklynart.com/">Joe Amrhein</a>'s affectionate reading of the detritus of art criticism at Roebling Hall<br />
390 Wythe</p>
