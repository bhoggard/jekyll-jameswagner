<p>While looking at this shot on my screen I was trying to decide whether to post what would just be another Lower East Side cloud picture.  Then I thought of an excuse:  I would say something about how if clouds were alive and sang they might have a chance of displacing birds in my personal hierarchy of the divine.  At that moment I noticed my simple cloud scene included the tiny fuzzy shape of a bird in flight and my question about its banality vanished.</p>

<p>For me it's still all about the birds.</p>
