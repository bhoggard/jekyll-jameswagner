<p>Michelangelo Signorile <a href="http://www.newsday.com/templates/misc/printstory.jsp?slug=ny%2Dvpsig023201843apr02&section=%2Fnews%2Fopinion">reports</a> that, in the midst of the real business of the military, its peak period, actual war, once again the Pentagon has decided that gays are too useful to be thrown out.</p>

<blockquote>
<p>Rather than speedily drumming out gays based on rumors or overheard declarations - the essence of the "don't ask, don't tell" policy - the military in some cases even appears intent on proving service people aren't gay, even after the individuals claim to be.</p>
</blockquote>
