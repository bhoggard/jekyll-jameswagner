<p>31 Grand has this wonderful piece by <a href="http://bloggy.com/mt/archives/005661.html">Carol "Riot" Kane</a> in their self-described "summer [group] show", titled <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/2/2773">"other worlds".</a>  While I can't say the installation isn't summery [I don't know anymore what a summer show is - if I ever did], I wouldn't describe the work as particularly light.  And no, that's not a criticism.</p>

<p>The other artists heating up the space on Grand Street this month are Fanny Bostrom, Magalie Guérin, Mara Herdmann, Francesca Lo Russo, Christa Parravani, and Anthony Pontius</p>
