<p>Some of <a href="http://kristen-jensen.com">Kristen Jensen</a>'s sculpture is currently part of an interesting group show, '<a href="http://www.wallspacegallery.com/gallery.html?id=270">The Curve</a>', at <a href="http://www.wallspacegallery.com/main.html">Wallspace Gallery</a> in Chelsea. The works are shown above, almost certainly in the artist's own elegant arrangement.  They are made of porcelain or stoneware, with the exception of the piece on the left, whose materials are described as campfire ashes and brass.  They are titled, clockwise from the left, 'Portable Black Hole', 'Steady, Steady Man', 'Dad Nose', 'Shim', and 'Bell'.</p>

<p>The other artists in the show, which closes January 14, are Jan Groover, Zachary Leener, Rebecca Morris, Monique Mouton, and Matt Paweski.</p>
