<p>The "homeland security" bill accepted by Republican-Democrats yesterday does <a href="http://www.newsday.com/templates/misc/printstory.jsp?slug=ny%2Dussecu133001681nov13&section=%2Fnews%2Fnationworld%2Fnation">not include</a> a provision for the creation of an independent commission to investigate the circumstances leading up to Sept. 11, although in September the Senate had actually overwhelmingly agreed to add such a commission to the bill.</p>

<p>Some people had this strange idea that it would be useful to know how the terrorists could have been so successful, but others must have a very good reason to ensure that we never know what happened.</p>
