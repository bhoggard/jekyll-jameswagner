<p>KING JOSIAH'S is surely the cleanest and most beautiful hot dog cart in the city of New York.  Note the condiments.   We spotted Josiah, and a few of his friends and customers, parked at the curb on the northwest corner of 8th Avenue and 14th Street tonight at 11 o'clock.  Frankfurters on a bun still only $1 each, even with all this style.</p>
