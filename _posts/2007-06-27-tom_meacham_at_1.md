<p>Tom Meacham's work is always about much more than what first meets the eye, and his <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/1/4848">current show</a> at 5BE is only the <a href="/2006/01/the_group_at_ol.html">latest</a> demonstration of his subtlety and intelligence.</p>

<p>The patterns on the canvases are sometimes ink jet and sometimes tape, and only two of them are actually <em>painted</em>.   That stack of stretched canvases isn't just a stack of stretched canvases, and the seemingly-unrelated sculpture in the center of the gallery does not reveal much of its story to the casual visitor.</p>

<p>Sometimes an installation's instruction sheet really is a welcome collaborator, and its necessity not an indictment of the strength of the work itself.  Of course it helps if the stuff is beautiful to begin with.</p>
