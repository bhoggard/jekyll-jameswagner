<p><a href="http://www.hort.uconn.edu/plants/a/amecan/amecan1.html">Our tree </a>arrives wednesday, and I feel like an expectant father!  I'm sure what follows then will be like a new career.  I've been without a garden since leaving the little 1760 house in Providence. While New York certainly has its compensations elsewise, if it's not <i>generally</i> imcomparable, I've still missed the garden in Rhode Island, perhaps most of all.</p>

<p>Well, it would be pretty cool if now I could reproduce at least one of the four working fireplaces I had on <a href="http://www.providenceri.com/Neighborhoods/foxpoint.html">Transit Street</a>, but that really would be quite a project, what with four apartments stacked above us here, and even then we wouldn't know where to store two cords of wood!</p>
