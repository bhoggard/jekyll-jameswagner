<p>If you're within earshot of this post - and of the Zwirner gallery on West 24th Street - you still have a chance to see one of the most beautiful shows of the year:  "<a href="http://www.artcat.com/exhibits/10794">Primary Atmospheres: Works from California 1960-1970</a>" closes this afternoon at 6.</p>

<p>The ten artists included in this museum-quality show are Peter Alexander, Larry Bell, Laddie John Dill, Robert Irwin, Craig Kauffman, John McCracken, Helen Pashgian, James Turrell, De Wain Valentine, and Doug Wheeler.</p>

<p>Awesome.</p>
