<p>It was just past midnight, midweek, and three excited friends were returning to Manhattan from Bushwick.  I looked up from our conversation for a moment and spotted this bank of passengers sitting across from us.  They were as wonderful, intriguing, smart, colorful and beautiful as every other group on the train I saw that night, or on any other night.</p>

<p>I love the subway;  I love Brooklyn;  I love New York.</p>
