<p>Last night I accompanied Barry to the <a href="http://goruco.com/">Gotham Ruby Conference (GORUCO)</a> afterparty.   I was his arm candy, we explained to anyone who asked about my [technical] orientation.  It was a beautiful night to be on the water, with a quarter moon above and under our feet a boat filled with some very nice and very smart people (including some <em>real</em> eye candy of all kinds).   When we neared the Statue of Liberty the skies exploded in a terrific fireworks display set off from a barge anchored just off Ellis Island.  Although this is the weekend of the 43rd anniversary of Stonewall, the occasion, and the sponsor, of these particular pyrotechnics was actually <a href="http://www.girlscouts100nj.org/girl-scouts-nj-the-big-celebration.html">The B.I.G. Celebration</a>.</p>

<p>I captured some more conventional shots, but this is the one which captured me, and it needed no Photoshopping.  Fireworks are abstractions anyway;  that's why most of us prefer them to bombs.</p>
