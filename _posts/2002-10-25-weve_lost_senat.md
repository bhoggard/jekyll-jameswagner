<p><a href="http://www.commondreams.org/">Senator Paul Wellstone</a>, his wife Sheila, daughter Marcia, and five others<br />
were killed today in a small plane crash near Eveleth, Minnesota.</p>

<p>The Senator was one of our most courageous and progressive legislators, and we have very very few.</p>

<p>His death is a tremendous <a href="http://www.alternet.org/story.html?StoryID=14384">tragedy</a> for the nation and the world.</p>
