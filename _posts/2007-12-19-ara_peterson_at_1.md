<p>four views of <a href="http://www.arapeterson.com/">Ara Peterson</a>'s site-specific wall relief inside one of the most popular containers of "Art Positions" at Art Basel Miami Beach last week.</p>

<p>This installation, of hand-painted, laser-cut birch plywood, glass mirrors and space, was arranged by <a href="http://www.johnconnellypresents.com/news/view/366">John Connelly Presents</a>.</p>

<p>It's my birthday, so although I already <a href="/2006/01/jim_drain_and_a.html">like</a> Peterson's work a lot, I have another good reason to put up these images today:  It's a very happy piece.  I got some great flowers this morning, but no balloons;  these colors and shapes will do very well instead.</p>
