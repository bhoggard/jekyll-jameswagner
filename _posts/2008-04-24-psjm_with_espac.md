<p>I was delighted with the quality of the installations at Pulse of <a href="http://www.espacioliquido.net/">Espacio Líquido</a>, a new Spanish gallery located in Gijón on the central Asturian coast.  They look like they'll be worth watching.</p>

<p>The images above can't begin to describe the project of just one element of the gallery's presence in New York last month.  My few words can't do much more but I can say that <a href="http://www.psjm.es/">PSJM</a> is a Madrid-based collaborative formed by Pablo San José and Cynthia Viera.   Tricked out as a shiny, sexy commercial brand itself, PSJM offers a critique of both global capitalism and market-based art.   "Made by Slaves for Free People" - so went the title of the pair's <a href="http://www.riflemaker.org/s-PSJM">show</a> at London's Riflemaker earlier this year.</p>
