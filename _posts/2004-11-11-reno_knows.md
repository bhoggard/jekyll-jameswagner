Of course!</p>

<p>Inside on the dashboard was a small stack of her DVD, "<a href="http://www.citizenreno.com/index_rebel.html">Rebel Without a Pause</a>."  Is our hero tempting the culturally and politically savvy thief, or just advertising?</p>
