<p>I guess we made it just in time.  But we didn't, really.  It's gone as of yesterday, and we only saw a few minutes of the screening.  "Butch Queen Realness with a Twist in Pastel Colors", is described as an ongoing video program curated by <a href="http://www.johnconnellypresents.com/avaf.htm">Assume Vivid Astro Focus</a> and it was being shown for one short month in the <a href="http://www.johnconnellypresents.com/current.htm">John Connelly Presents</a> gallery annex.</p>

<p>This wonderful program should be installed permanently in theatres accessible to people everywhere in the world.  Yes, I think the program would have to change over time, although perhaps it should only be lengthened, even though I understand it's already four hours long.</p>

<p>Together the four works whose stills are shown above are on the screen less than eleven minutes.  I wish we could go back and camp out for a while;   After these last few weeks, I'm definitely ready to shut down the outside world.</p>
