<p>I have little idea what this is about, but it's the piece I remember best from all the work I saw in a very interesting show of sculpture, "<a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/6/8542">Without Walls</a>", at Museum 52.   I don't know if it tells us anything about the artist himself;  maybe Michael just found this stack somewhere on the street, with or without the needle lying on top, and decided to <em>mark</em> it with his signature.  And then maybe not.</p>

<p>For me the important thing is that I'm preternaturally attracted to it, and would be even if <a href="http://www.papermag.com/blogs/2007/05/porn_star_roman_ragazzi_and_pe.php">Roman Ragazzi</a> were not staring up from the floor.  It also reminds me of the <a href="/2004/03/the_next_chinat.html">happy</a> happenstance that Mahalchick has another solo show opening at CANADA on Friday, titled, "<a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/6/8738">For What It's Worth</a>".</p>
