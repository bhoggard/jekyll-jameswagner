<blockquote>
<p>"He has a little soul, to rock n' roll</p>

<p>Every record that he touches turns to gold</p>

<p>He's well conducted, self-instructed</p>

<p>His styles were plied, heavily constructed</p>

<p>Mechanically inclined, and if you don't mind</p>

<p>We add spice to your life, time after time</p>

<p>And think about times, where he's a long laster</p>

<p>We rock our rhymes for the Jam-Master."</p>

<p>"Jam Master Jammin'"  (1985)</p>
</blockquote>
