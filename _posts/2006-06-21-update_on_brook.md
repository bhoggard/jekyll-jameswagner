Elizabeth Louise Vigée Le Brun <em>Innocence seeking refuge in the Arms of Justice</em>  1779</p>

<p>The eighteen students whose Master of Fine Arts thesis show was summarily <a href="/2006/05/new_york_city_s.html">shut down</a> on May 4th by a parks official, with the work removed and damaged by their school shortly thereafter, filed suit this month against the City of New York, the NYC Parks Department and Brooklyn College, citing First Amendment violations and property damages.</p>

<p>I would expect this case to be a no-brainer for any court, but I no longer have the naive confidence in American justice with which I was brought up.</p>

<p>For details on the suit, see the PLAN C(ENSORED) <a href="http://plancensored.blogspot.com/2006/06/brooklyn-college-mfa-students-sue-nyc.html">site</a>.</p>
