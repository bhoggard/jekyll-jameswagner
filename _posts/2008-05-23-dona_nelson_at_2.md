<p>I'm thinking Dona Nelson's work is looking better and better, but I'm sure the reasons are simple:  I'm seeing more and more of it and Thomas Erben is such a great curator.  Like <a href="/2006/09/dona_nelson_at.html">the last show</a> he mounted for the artist this one is something of a closely-edited mini-retrospective.  The <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/1/6974">current</a> installation, "in situ: paintings 1973 - present",  remains on 26th Street through May 31.</p>
