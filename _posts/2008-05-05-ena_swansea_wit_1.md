<p><a href="http://www.andreschlechtriem.com/"><br />
Andre Schlechtriem</a> showed some beautiful work by Ena Swansea at Volta.  The oil and graphite paintings were very beautiful, but I think I was even more excited about the large-scale serigraph print portfolio, "4 Seasons".</p>

<p>Because of light problems inside the space at the fair I don't have a decent image of my own documenting any painting from the show.   All of this gorgeous stuff has to be seen in person to be properly appreciated, but still I wanted to show a painting here too so I looked on line for a good copy of a work I found particularly interesting.   Swansea's mammoth study, "Theory of Relativity", fills the bill very nicely, even if it is from a few years back and wasn't at Volta.</p>
