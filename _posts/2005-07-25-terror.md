<p>The gang of men who accosted and chased a terrified young electrician  into the tube where they shot five bullets into his head after he tripped and fell onto the floor of a car filled with people were <strong>plainclothes</strong> policemen.</p>

<p><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jean_Charles_de_Menezes">Jean Charles de Menezes</a> entered Britain with student visa;  he may or may not have still been a legal resident three days ago, but on the other hand, especially by post-9/11 standards, it's clear he was not perfectly white.</p>
