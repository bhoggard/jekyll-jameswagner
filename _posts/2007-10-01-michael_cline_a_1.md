<p>In time, Michael Cline's jaw-dropping <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/1/5455">show</a> of oils at Daniel Reich, "Folks", may be recorded as a cultural benchmark, both aesthetic and social, for offering us such a peculiar and powerful window onto the darker and neglected side of this urban moment.  It certainly will not be forgotten by those who experience the paintings.</p>

<p>These sacred/profane <em>altarpieces</em> go where the photographer's art cannot.</p>
