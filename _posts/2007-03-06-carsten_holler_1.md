Carsten Höller <em>Kabine</em> 2003-2007 acrylic glass 54" x 18" x 21" [installation view]</p>

<p><a href="http://www.caseykaplangallery.com/menu.html">Casey Kaplan</a> had this seductive piece  by Carsten Höller lying on the floor outside the gallery's <a href="http://www.thearmoryshow.com/">Armory</a> booth, just waiting for this blogger nut, with his obsession about minimalist transportation designs, to pass by and scoop it up - metaphorically, of course.  I love this piece, but I probably cannot account for my passion in art terms alone.</p>

<p>For those who can't quite make it out in this picture, inside the teardrop-shaped capsule there is the suggestion of a perfectly-reclined seat awaiting its lone time-and-space passenger/operator.</p>
