<p>The headline refers to a member of the <a href="http://www.palsolidarity.org/">International Solidarity Movement</a> (ISM) and it is not hyperbole.  The policy of the Sharon government is to discourage foreign defenders of the rights and dignity of the Palestinian people it has reduced to the status of chattel.  Today it simply escalated the means it employs and a young American woman, Rachel Corrie, was <a href="http://www.palsolidarity.org/rachel.htm">murdered by military bulldozer</a> as she attempted to defend a Palestinian home.  The half dozen photographs on this site are truly horrifying.</p>

<blockquote>
<p>The Israeli Army are attempting to dishonour her memory by claiming <br />
that Rachel was killed accidentally when she ran in front of the <br />
bulldozer.  Eye-witnesses to the murder insist that this is totally <br />
untrue.  Rachel was sitting in the path of the bulldozer as it <br />
advanced towards her.  When the bulldozer refused to stop or turn <br />
aside she climbed up onto the mound of dirt and rubble being <br />
gathered in front of it wearing a fluorescent jacket to look <br />
directly at the driver who kept on advancing.  The bulldozer <br />
continued to advance so that she was pulled under the pile of dirt <br />
and rubble.  After she had disappeared from view the driver kept <br />
advancing until the bulldozer was completely on top of her.  The <br />
driver did not lift the bulldozer blade and so she was crushed <br />
beneath it.  Then the driver backed off and the seven other ISM <br />
activists taking part in the action rushed to dig out her body.  An <br />
ambulance rushed her to A-Najar hospital where she died.</p>

<p>. . . .</p>

<p>Rachel had been staying in Palestinian homes threatened with illegal demolition, and today Rachel was standing with other non-violent international activists in front of a home scheduled for illegal demolition. According to witnesses, Rachel was run over twice by the Israeli military bulldozer in its process of demolishing the Palestinian home. Witnesses say that Rachel was clearly visible to the bulldozer driver, and was doing nothing to provoke an attack.</p>
</blockquote>

<p>This should, and just possibly might, seal the fate of the Sharon government and possible future imitators, but also its Washington partner in these murders.</p>
