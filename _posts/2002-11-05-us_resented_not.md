<p>In the context of a <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2002/11/05/opinion/L05FRIE.html">letter</a> to the editor responding to a really problematic <u>NYTimes</u> OP-Ed piece by Thomas L. Friedman, the writer includes a compelling european view of the U.S. today.</p>

<blockquote>
<p>The United States is the friendly but overweight neighbor who owns a big house and a big car, has a pile of junk in his backyard, thinks he owns the block and walks around waving his gun. This neighbor doesn't bring on envy, but he sure does raise some eyebrows.</p>
</blockquote>
