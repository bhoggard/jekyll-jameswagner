<p>Zurich's <a href="http://www.roemerapotheke.ch/index.html">Römerapotheke</a> showed these and other paintings and drawings by <a href="http://www.roemerapotheke.ch/kuenstler,marcel_gaehler,34.html">Marcel Gähler</a> inside its camp at Scope.  They were among the most beautiful and terrifying things I say among all the paintings I encountered that week.</p>

<p>This <a href="/2006/03/simon_english_w.html">excellent gallery</a>'s own text includes the note:</p>

<blockquote>
<p>His painting drives us towards the limits of our perception. It makes it disconcertingly clear that seeing nothing does not imply that nothing is there.</p>
</blockquote>
