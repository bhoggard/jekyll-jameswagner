<p>This is the kind of show in the kind of space which just might save Chelsea from SOHOification.  A lot of us want to be surprised and excited in between too many sessions nodding at the almost predictable and the pretty slick - even when much of the predictable and the slick is also very good.</p>

<p>Exit is an exciting and very young and very shy, skateboarding and bike-culture-centered, Chicago-graduate-art-school-dropout, Brooklyn artist interested in fashion and birds, with a particular and very fashionable obsession with bird flu.  All of the beautiful drawings in the current show at <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/1/2519">Magnan Emrich Contemporary</a> on 28th Street deal with the impact of the long-predicted epidemic upon a world dominated by Miuccia Prada, Anna Wintour and their peers.</p>

<p>Very cool stuff, and totally infectious.</p>
