<p>Andres Laracuente was <a href="/2007/02/matthew_lutzkin_1.html">another</a> young American shown by the Parisian gallery <a href="http://yukikokawase.free.fr/">Yukiko Kawase</a> during last week's <a href="http://www.divafair.com/ny_07/">DIVA</a> fair.  In the voyeuristic video from which the two stills shown above were taken the good-humored and obliging artist is seen following the directives of an off-screen balloon-popping fetishist he had located on Craig's List.</p>

<p>In another video of his seen on Sunday afternoon, "The In-crowd Tickle", a tickling-fetish top is seen interacting with the artist, this time in <em>front</em> of the camera.</p>
