<p>Noah Taylor, the actor who portrays Adolf Hitler in <a href="http://www.salon.com/ent/movies/int/2002/09/09/cusack/">"Max"</a> explains his approach to an intimidating assignment in a <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2003/01/10/nyregion/10BOLD.html">tiny item</a> of the <u>NYTimes</u> today, "Seeking the Dictator Within."</p>

<blockquote>
<p>Speaking of obstacles, there were some lines that must have presented a challenge.  "'I'm Hitler, Adolf Hitler.' It was hard to say that without sounding like, 'Bond, James Bond,' " Mr. Taylor said. "But John [Cusack] had the hardest one: 'C'mon, Hitler, I'll buy you a lemonade.'"</p>
</blockquote>
