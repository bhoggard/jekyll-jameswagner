<p><strong>ADDENDUM:</strong>  [April 21] "<a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2007/04/21/opinion/21herrera.html?_r=1&oref=slogin">A Terrorist Goes Free</a>"</p>

<p>Terrorist Luis Posada Carriles, wanted in Cuba and Venezuela [but not in the United States] for the 1976 bombing of a Cuban airliner that killed 73 people was <a href="http://news.yahoo.com/s/nm/20070419/ts_nm/cuban_exile_dc_3">released on bail</a> in New Mexico today.  He has been held in the U.S. on immigration charges since 1975 and will theoretically be in El Paso for a trial which is scheduled to finally begin May 11.</p>

<p>Posada Carriles was trained by the CIA and has ties to the Bush family.</p>
