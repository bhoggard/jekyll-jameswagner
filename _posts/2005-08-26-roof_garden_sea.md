<p>It's MoMA's new aquisition, not ours, but it sure is an exciting chair.</p>

<p>For a few seconds I fantisized that I'd found the perfect sculptural seating for our roof garden.  Orange on green.  Fantastic.  It could accomodate two very good friends at once, but we'd probably have to commission a nice cushion or two and it would need a cover for bad weather.  None of this is a problem however, since I'm sure we can't afford it anyway.  This piece is handmade, and since according to the label it was donated to MoMA by its Chairman, billionaire Ron Lauder [presumably for the <a href="http://www.moma.org/exhibitions/2004/arch_design_11-20-04.html">Architecture and Design Collection</a>], even if it isn't unique it had to cost a bundle.</p>
