<p>No, it's not a political post this time, but the name of a show.  We're finally going to get a chance to see our friend David Driver and a gaggle of other brilliants in "People Are Wrong!" at Joe's Pub this saturday!</p>

<blockquote>
<p>People Are Wrong! is a manic and joyful celebration of melody and musical fable. Based on a true story, this original cautionary tale of a charismatic cult leader masquerading as a landscape artist in a rural upstate town is told entirely in song.</p>

<p>Starring the brilliant downtown chanteur David Driver (Rent, Fire at Keaton's Bar & Grill),  John Flansburgh (They Might Be Giants), Maggie Moore (Hedwig & The Angry Inch) and Chris Anderson (Muckafurguson), People are Wrong! Introduces the songwriting talents of Julia Greenberg and Robin Goldwasser to the musical stage.</p>

<p>"It had its way with me and left me wanting more"<br />
-John Cameron Mitchell, creator of Hedwig</p>

<p>"A cult above the others... Zingy!"<br />
-James Rado, lyricist of Hair</p>
</blockquote>

<p>For more details and titilations, see <a href="http://www.peoplearewrong.net">their website</a>.</p>
