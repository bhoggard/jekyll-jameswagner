<p>The more immediate, political evil of "<a href="https://jameswagner.com/index.php?m=200211#522">family values</a>" affects all of us, whether our own families are "valued" or not.  In Florida, the Bush family has been <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2002/11/26/politics/26FLOR.html">helped</a> by the Rehnquist family, even as each family helps itself.</p>

<blockquote>
<p>At the request of Gov. Jeb Bush's office, the inspector general of the Health and Human Services Department ordered delays in a federal audit of Florida's pension fund that ensured that the review would not be completed before Governor Bush won re-election, officials said today.</p>

<p>Congress is investigating the delays sought by the inspector general, Janet Rehnquist, daughter of Chief Justice William H. Rehnquist.</p>
</blockquote>

<p>I'm not making this up.</p>
