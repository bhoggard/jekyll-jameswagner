<p>As <a href="http://bloggy.com/mt/archives/006587.html">the chief</a> says, "we're still working on a few features and tweaking the design", but the <a href="http://www.artcal.net/">new ArtCal</a> site is now live.   By next week at this time, with the opening of New York's vigorous fall gallery season, the nearly <a href="/2004/11/artcal_steps_up.html">three-year-old</a> site will be displaying more shows than ever before, but sorting them out will be easier than ever before.  Some additional features have been added, and there will be more to come.</p>

<p>Go <a href="http://www.artcal.net/">see</a> for yourself.</p>
