<p>I don't have any details, mostly because this is a view of an open portfolio of an artist whose work the people of Seattle's very interesting artists-run <a href="http://platformgallery.com/">Platform Gallery</a> had first come across just prior to the Miami fairs.  His name is Marc Dombrosky and, as it was described to me, he hand-embroiders the images or text of pieces of paper which he might find discarded just about anywhere.</p>

<p>As you might expect, the results vary, but the level of their interest begins with, well, . . . just fascinating, and continues toward  something like profound, even sublime.</p>

<p>Disclosure:  I have accepted an invitation from the gallery to do catalogue interviews for their current exhibition.</p>
