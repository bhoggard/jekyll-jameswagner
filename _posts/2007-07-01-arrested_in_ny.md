<p>An AP <a href="http://www.newsday.com/news/local/wire/newyork/ny-bc-ny--activistarrested0701jul01,0,352044.story?coll=ny-region-apnewyork">story</a> in <u>Newsday</u> reports that <a href="http://www.revbilly.com/">Reverend Billy</a> was arrested Friday night while loudly reciting the First Amendment to police.</p>

<p>Could anything make it more clear <a href="/2007/06/radical_homosex_2.html">what's going on</a> in this city?  It's time for all New Yorkers to form a larger <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Critical_Mass"><em>critical mass</em></a> of resistance to this dangerous lunacy before we've lost our liberties for ever.</p>

<p><a href="http://gothamist.com/2007/06/30/reverend_billy.php">Gothamist</a> tells us that a press release the blog received after the arrest of a man whose day job consists largely of exhorting people to abandon the products of large corporations and mass media, observes that "<strong>while the NYPD surrounded and intimidated last night's Critical Mass cyclists, a line of several hundred shoppers formed just across the street to purchase the new iPhone, blocking pedestrian traffic and forcing people to walk in the street.</strong>"  Whoa!  This is all way, way beyond irony.</p>
