<p>Watching that stupid little fool, inarguably the most powerful man on the planet, propped up in the white House behind a podium with its modern speaking tube mumbling, and as if sedated, the same phrases over and over again may have been the scariest experience of my life.  I have always tried to avoid watching him, and with great success until tonight.  This time the prospect had something of the attraction of a choice seat for the proverbial train wreck, and I just had to be there, since it bode well to be a doozy.  It was.</p>

<p>"Pay no attention to the man behind the curtains!"</p>
