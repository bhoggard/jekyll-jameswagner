<p>I know it's almost two months away, but if your calendar fills up like my calendar does, and if you already know how worthy this operation is, you'll appreciate knowing now that the 2008 <a href="http://www.nurtureart.org/">NURTUREart</a> benefit is scheduled for October 27th.  It's also an terrific opportunity to bring home some terrific art, so put it in your calendars:  As <a href="http://bloggy.com/2008/07/nurtureart_benefit_s.html">Barry</a> says on Bloggy, "You know any group that <a href="/2007/05/nurtureart_bene_1.html">honored</a> James and me at the last one has excellent taste".</p>

<p>Oh, and the bash will be in Manhattan, at <a href="http://www.jamescohan.com/">James Cohan Gallery</a> on West 26th Street.  More details as we get closer to the date.</p>
