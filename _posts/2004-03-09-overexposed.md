<p>I like <a href="http://www.nynewsday.com/"><u>Newsday</u></a>, and I guess they like me.  Last week pictures of me or my signs at a same-sex marriage rights demonstration appeared in their pages twice in one day.  Yesterday Barry and I were the subject of a column on our outrage about MTA disaster preparedness, and today they're including an edited version of my letter to the editor on the same subject.</p>

<p>Just coincidence.  Still, maybe I should lie low for a while if I don't want to be shut out because of overexposure.  It's going to be a nosiy year.  I just might still have something important to say.</p>
