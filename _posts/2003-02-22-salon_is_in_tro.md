<p>In an open letter to the online magazine, <a href="http://atrios.blogspot.com/2003_02_16_atrios_archive.html#90359988">Atrios</a> shows that he understands at least part of the reason they aren't making <a href="http://www.salon.com/letters/editor/2003/02/22/raise_limbaugh/index.html">enough money</a> to survive.</p>

<blockquote>
<p>. . . I have to say that your continued promotion of incoherent lunatics such as Andrew Sullivan doesn't help the cause much. Aside from making Salon look completely foolish, Andy's presence in your magazine really destroys whatever warm fuzzy feelings your supposedly liberal readership might have. I mean, hey, I could be wrong - I don't have information about your subscribers or your site traffic, but I have a hard time believing that people are really going to pay to read essentially the same drivel - "LIBERALS STUPID AND BAD AND TREASONOUS" - that they can read for free over in his own little sandbox. I subscribe, I encourage others to do so, but I can't really fault their reasons for not doing so.</p>
</blockquote>
