<p><a href="http://www.redbullmusicacademy.com/ART.15.0.html?act_aced=112&ex_page_id=39">Rä di Martino</a> has a wonderful video in a terrific group show, "The Mind/Body Problem" at <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/7/1731">Artists Space</a>.  I wish every film packed as much into a feature's length as this Möbius-Strip-like piece does in just seven minutes.  I stood in the room and watched it, I think, four times.  It's still playing somewhere inside my head.</p>
