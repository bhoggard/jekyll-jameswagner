<p>Like, we can afford to <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2002/11/15/national/15ARMY.html">throw away</a> linguists, arabic or otherwise, at a time like this, or any time!</p>

<blockquote>
<p>Nine Army linguists, including six trained in Arabic, have been dismissed from the military because they are gay, even as the military faces a critical shortage of translators and interpreters for the war on terrorism, gay rights advocates say.</p>

<p>Seven of the soldiers were discharged after telling superiors that they are gay; two others were caught together after curfew, said Steve Ralls, spokesman for the Servicemembers Legal Defense Network, a group that defends homosexuals in the military.</p>
</blockquote>
