<p>Could any report on this, the sixth day of the slaughter in this unjust war, be more revealing of the failure of American journalism, even of America?  This is the actual <u>NYTimes</u> headline, spread in two horizontal lines across all six columns of the front page of the "Late Edition" this morning:<b></p>

<blockquote>
<p>ALLIES AND IRAQIS BATTLE ON 2 FRONTS; <br />
20 AMERICANS DEAD OR MISSING, 5O HURT</p>
</blockquote>

<p></b>This goes well beyond the effrontery in the famous pre-World War II headline in the <u>Times</u> of London: "Fog in Channel: Continent isolated."</p>

<p>Unless "the newpaper of record" is reporting that after five days no Iraqis are dead or hurt, the <u>Times</u> is joining our fascist regime, and most Americans, in refusing to recognize the humanity of our victims.</p>
