<p>This year's NURTUREart benefit will be on October 12th, at Claire Oliver in Chelsea.  Barry and I will be there.  It's one our <a href="http://www.nurtureart.org/nurtureart/index.php?ptr=page&guid=13">favorite</a> non-profits and one of the most fun events of the year (okay, <a href="/2007/06/last_chance_to.html">they like us</a> too, and the price is right).  It's also a terrific opportunity to acquire some great stuff while at the same time supporting both emerging artists and emerging curators.</p>

<p>Raising money for the arts is obviously tougher than ever this year, but this event offers a precious opportunity to forget for a night that <a href="/2009/09/new_museum_commits_s.html">not everyone</a> believes that great art can exist even when it hasn't been bought by the right people.</p>

<p>For the first time ever, all of the works which will be available at the benefit can be viewed on line.  Have a sneak preview <a href="http://benefit09.nurtureart.org/">here</a>, and join us on West 26th Street less on <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Columbus_Day">Día de la Raz</a>.</p>

<p>Also, note that tomorrow, Wednesday will be the last day to take advantage of the early bird admission price, so hurry down to your local internet line.</p>
