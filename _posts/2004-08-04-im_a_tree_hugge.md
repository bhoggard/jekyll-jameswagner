<p>These wonderful creatures could easily turn me into an animist.  These palms were waving above the high terrace of the <a href="http://www.lacma.org/">Los Angeles County Museum of Art</a> this afternoon.</p>

<p>But, at least on the surface, the <a href="http://www.lacma.org/info/press/beyondgeomPR.htm">current</a> featured exhibition, "Beyond Geometry: Experiments in Form 1940s&#8722;70s," could hardly be more removed from these beautiful sentinals outside.  It's a stunning show, even if I somehow missed the argument of its curatorial premise.</p>
