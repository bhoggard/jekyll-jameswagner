<p>The tiny Envoy gallery has currently has a show, "<a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/1/1995">(Un)masked</a>", which includes the work of three artists or artist-collaboratives, <a href="http://www.crackerfarm.com/flash.html">Crackerfarm</a>, <a href="http://anp.thing.net/artists/duncan.html">George Duncan</a> and <a href="http://www.featureinc.com/artists_bios/linn.ht">Judy Linn</a>.</p>

<p>The two wonderful silver gelatin prints shown above in a detail of the gallery's installation are Judy Linn's <em>A saint in any form</em> and <em>Robert Mapplethorpe in bed at the Chelsea #2</em>, both from the 1970's, while her friends Patti Smith and Robert Mapplethorpe were living together in the Chelsea Hotel.</p>
