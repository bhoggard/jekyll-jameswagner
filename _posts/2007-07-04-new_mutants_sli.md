<p>Once again <a href="http://www.canadanewyork.com/">Canada</a> manages to put on a show which local art fans will miss at their own peril, or at least at the risk of losing out on some good serious fun.  <a href="http://www.pictureboxinc.com/">Dan Nadel</a> curated the gallery's <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/6/4992">"New Mutants"</a>, a show of work by Melissa Brown, Brian Chippendale,  Julie Doucet, C.F., Trenton Doyle Hancock, Ben Jones, Amy Lockhart, Sakura Maku, Frank Santoro, Patrick Smith and Michael Williams.  Yes, <a href="/2007/02/michael_william.html">that</a> Michael Williams.</p>
