<p>Is <a href="http://news.bbc.co.uk/hi/english/world/americas/newsid_2007000/2007679.stm">this</a> what the F.B.I. had in mind when it warned us about <a href="http://www.cnn.com/2002/US/05/26/terror.threats/index.html">light aircraft</a>?</p>

<blockquote>
<p>An attempt to honour a dead baseball fan's last wish went horribly wrong on Friday, forcing the evacuation of Safeco Field, the home of the Seattle Mariners baseball team, amid fears of a bio-terror attack.</p>
</blockquote>

<p>The plane dropped a package which exploded on impact, spraying the area with a "mystery white powder."  It later turned out that the package contained the ashes of a devout Mariners fan who had wanted his remains cast over the field.</p>
