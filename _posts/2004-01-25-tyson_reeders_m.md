<p>His first solo show in New York was an attraction both for the work and for the smart, beautiful crowd Tyson Reeder was able to attract to the <a href="http://www.danielreichgallery.com/index2.html">Daniel Reich Gallery</a> on one of the coldest nights of the winter.</p>

<p>Reed's show and his current home are both called "Milwaukee".  His parents are at home in the somewhat less zany precincts of eastern Michigan.</p>

<p>Because of my parents' history, and my own childhood, I thought I knew a little about Milwaukee, but <a href="http://www.jsonline.com/onwisconsin/arts/oct03/177966.asp">this</a> was something very new.  The paintings on canvas and the drawings do not really open up in reproduction, but save most of their textural beauty for a visit with the naked eye.</p>
