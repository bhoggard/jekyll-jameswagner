Joe Ovelman, <em>Two Walls</em> 2003, guerilla installation</p>

<p>If you missed Joe Ovelman's walls in Chelsea last month, and if you want to see more of the work I've talked about in the <a href="/2003/02/joe_ovelmans_qu.html">past</a> on this site, stop by Oliver Kamm's <a href="http://www.5begallery.com/content.php?mode=gallery&section_id=22">5BE Gallery</a> by November 15, when the current small group show closes.</p>

<p>Ovelman has covered most of one wall of the gallery with many of the arresting images with which he had <a href="https://jameswagner.com/gallery/joewall1">earlier</a> wheatpasted the plywood on either 25th Street or 10th Avenue, and I don't think anyone has torn them off the plaster yet.</p>

<p>5BE Gallery is located on the second floor at 504 West 22nd Street, just west of 10th Avenue.</p>
