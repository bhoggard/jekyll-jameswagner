<p><a href="http://www.searspeyton.com/artist/artistn_ins.php3?artist=5&tip=0">Sears-Peyton</a> was <a href="/2007/02/roz_leibowitz_a.html">also</a> showing some beautiful work at the <a href="http://www.reddotfair.com/">Red Dot</a> fair by the Texas-born artist <a href="http://touchon.com/">Cecil Touchon</a> who now lives in Mexico, where he assembles these collages using cutouts of typographical elements in found street posters and roadside billboard papers which had been used, among other purposes, to advertise local wrestling or other festivals and events.</p>
