<p>The memorial for <a href="/2005/09/a_huge_transfer_1.html">Bill Bartman</a> will be celebrated at 2 pm on Saturday, November 5 in the Society of <a href="http://15stfriends.quaker.org/">Friends Meeting House</a>.  The beautiful, very simple brick 1860 <a href="http://www.nyc-architecture.com/GRP/GRP009.htm">building</a> is located off the western boundary of Stuyvesant Square.  See <a href="http://15stfriends.quaker.org/location.html">directions</a> and map.</p>

<p>Everyone is welcome, and yes, there will be cookies.</p>

<p>NOTE:  Anyone who might have one or more good photo images of Bill, and especially if they are of Bill with a group of people, is asked to call the <a href="http://www.artretran.com/">Art Resources Transfer</a> (A.R.T.) office at 212 564-2262.  A slide show loop is being assembled by his friends.</p>
