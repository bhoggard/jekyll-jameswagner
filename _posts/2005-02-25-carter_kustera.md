<p>Violence with a flair.  In his current <a href="http://www.lyonswiergallery.com/html/CurrentExhibition.html">show</a> at Lyonsweirgallery Carter Kustera finds a way to seriously address the commercial world's obsession with glamorizing violence without abandoning his own aesthetic - or his <a href="http://www.businessweek.com/bwdaily/dnflash/dec1999/nf91221c.htm">usual</a> good humor.</p>

<p>The show is titled, "Fabulous Anger," and these provocative works on paper will be up until March 12.</p>

<p>The press release on the gallery site tells us how we can become be an integral part of Kustera's art and wit.  He's also a really nice guy, which would be  pretty relevant to those who can accept this offer:</p>
