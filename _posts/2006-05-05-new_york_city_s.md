<p>Don't they ever learn?  NYC Parks authorities have abruptly shut down the MFA thesis exhibit for the art school of Brooklyn College/CUNY.</p>

<p>It will be interesting to see how the art collector successor to <a href="http://www.salon.com/news/feature/1999/10/02/giuliani/index.html">Giuliani</a>  deals with this one.  See Barry for <a href="http://bloggy.com/mt/archives/005553.html">the story</a> up to now.  There you will learn that <a href="http://plancensored.blogspot.com/">this dedicated blog</a> has been set up to provide continuously-updated information.</p>

<p>Pictured is the cover of the catalog for the Nazi's 1937 "Degenerate Art" exhibition.  I have a copy here at home, a very precious and tragic relic of a product of what I once thought was an alien mind.</p>
