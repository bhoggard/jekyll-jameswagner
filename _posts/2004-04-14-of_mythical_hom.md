<p>Look around.  These people have removed all joy from lives all over the planet since they seized power little over three years ago, but since there was obviously no pleasure in their dried-up Republican hearts anyway, they are oblivious to the cloud now hanging over the planet.  In fact, for those little superstitious minds the next life just can't come soon enough;  they're apparently willing to help their god-thing hurry it along.</p>
