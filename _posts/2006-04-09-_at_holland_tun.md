Holland Tunnel</a> has a remarkable show, "<em>Ménage à Trois</em>", of paintings by Tom Billings, Jacques Roch and Jennifer Toth.  It continues through the rest of this week.  Unfortunately the gallery seems to have lost its website and I didn't leave the little garden shed on South 3rd Street with any more information about the show or the artists.  Worse still, I can't find anything on line about Tom Billings.</p>
