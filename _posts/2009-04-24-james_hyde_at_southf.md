<p><a href="http://www.artcat.com/event/view/2/9006">This show</a> closes on Sunday.  It was a top pick on ArtCat, and was extended from its original closing date of April 19.  Barry and I were there in the middle of March.  We both agreed immediately that it was a terrific show, but I'd somehow forgotten to tell any one who might visit this site, and to post some representative images while I was at it.</p>

<p>I've always thought <a href="http://www.jameshyde.com/">James Hyde</a>'s work was terrific.  I once fell head over heels in love with a luscious, smallish, lipstick-red, wall-mounted cube* he had created which seemed to me to represent <em>painting</em>, in its purest, most fundamental form.  Hyde has never stood still, and the show at Southfirst certainly shows that he's still moving:   painted abstract shapes and structures joined with his own conceptual photographic images.</p>
