<p>You won't see these images if you head over to <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/6/6372">Canada</a> now (unless they've somehow escaped getting picked up by savvy collectors, and in that case you might want to ask to see them).  The show, <a href="/2005/04/brian_belott_at.html">Brian Belott</a>'s "<a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/6/5977">Swirly Music</a>", was extended a week, making it possible for Barry and I to catch it, but it's definitely gone now.</p>

<p>Three weeks later however these images are still swirling in my head, and that's why I'm uploading them here today.  They've waited long enough.</p>

<p>See Brent Burket, in the <a href="http://zine.artcal.net/2008/01/brian-belott-at-canada.php">ArtCal Zine</a>, for more on this seriously-whimsical show.</p>
