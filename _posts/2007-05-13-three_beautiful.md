<p>Spaniards are almost always beautiful, but the older men and women have a kind of grace not found in youth, even the youth of Iberia.  Not incidentally, they also dress much better than most of the kids and grandkids.</p>

<p>These three friends were sitting under the portico of a the Plaza Mayor in El Burgo de Osma last Wednesday afternoon.</p>
