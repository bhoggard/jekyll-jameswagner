<p>Ok, this should be the last log item coming out of the New Festival screenings, but it's a real <a href="http://members.aol.com/boychickmovie/about.html">winner</a>!  "Boychik" is a magical little film which was part of the most excellent Growing Pains shorts program.</p>

<p>It's tough recommending a short, since we're not likely to see it advertised at the multiplex, but follow your index finger (the reference will be clear to those who manage to see it) if you ever discover it on a program.</p>
