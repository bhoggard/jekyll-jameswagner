<p>These are images of five more short performances included in the <a href="http://catchseries.org/">Catch 30</a> night of the <a href="http://movementresearch.org/festival/08/">Movement Research Festival</a> I wrote about in my <a href="/2008/06/elliott_durko_l.html">previous post</a>.  The lighting was a challenge, and I didn't leave my seat, so my images are both compromised and few.</p>

<p>I think all of the ten pieces we saw were either excerpts, works in progress, or just tantalizing glimpses of more ambitious works.  I wish we could be given strings attached to [most of] these artists, to be sure we'd be alerted to what comes next.</p>
