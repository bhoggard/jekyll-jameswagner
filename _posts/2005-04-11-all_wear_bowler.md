<p>Six weeks ago I <a href="/2005/02/i_wont_give_any.html">wrote</a> about a terrific theatre piece at HERE, "<a href="http://allwearbowlers.com/">All Wear Bowlers</a>."  After a brief hiatis Trey Lyford and Geoff Sobelle are back with the same show.   Performances begin once <a href="http://67.89.63.10/here/website/showDetail.cfm?showID=929">again</a> on April 22.</p>

<p>If you missed it the first time around, you can still get to heaven, and it will cost you only $20, $17, maybe $15, or even $10, depending upon your status when you ask for tickets.</p>
