<p>Today was a slow day at the El Escorial.  Indeed, it was a day the palace/monastery was closed, but the huge terrace was pulsing with life during a lunch break for the students at the Real Colegio Alfonso XII.</p>

<p>By precisely 3 o'clock everyone had disappeared inside the doors and the terrace was empty.</p>
