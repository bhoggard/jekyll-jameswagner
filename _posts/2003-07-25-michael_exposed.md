<p>Now I know why the crowd was not quite as intimidating as I had expected at the fabulous <a href="http://www.snevil.com/subdermal/troll.html">Reverend Jen's Troll Museum</a> opening at <a href="http://printedmatter.org/index.cfm">Printed Matter</a> last night!</p>

<p>It wasn't because of an interruption in L train service this time.  <a href="http://www.acria.org/acria.html">ACRIA</a> was holding a benefit and juried exhibition at <a href="http://www.lehmannmaupin.com/">Lehmann Maupin Gallery</a> a few blocks away at the same time, and it wasn't just the free sparkling stuff in real glasses that had attracted the huge crowd.</p>

<p>They were almost giving away ($150, duh!) hot works by hot young artists picked by hot older artists - and for the benefit of a wonderful institution!  Everyone wins.</p>

<p>We had forgotten the time-sensitive nature of the event, so we arrived after just about everything had been sold.  Otherwise the two extraordinary <a href="http://lflgallery.com/orlyjeff.html">Orly Cogan</a> works reproduced above, which were scooped up in the first few minutes, might now be ours.  Orly's third image, not reproduced here, was titled, "Puppy Love."</p>

<p>The art will remain on the walls of the gallery until August 2.</p>
