<p>I love these little pieces.  Jared Lindsay Clark's small ceramic collage sculptures were a very special part of the booth of Richmond's <a href="http://www.adagallery.com/jared_clark.html">ADA Gallery</a> at Scope.  I can't say enough however, no, <em>literally</em>, since the artist's <a href="http://www.jaredlindsayclark.com/">site</a> uses flash and the gallery's link to Clark just isn't working.  But I did find <a href="http://jaredlindsayclark.blogspot.com/">this blog</a> which seems to belong to Clark.  It has some more views of these pieces, images of more work from this series, and a lot more stuff from the artist and his friends.</p>

<p>I love Richmond.  I haven't been there in ages, but it's getting harder to stay away.</p>
