<p><a href="http://www.guildgreyshkul.com/">Guild & Greyshkul</a> showed this wonderful paper creation by <a href="/2004/06/valerie_hegarty.html">Valerie Hegarty</a> in their booth at NADA Miami early this month.</p>

<p>Note that Hegarty seems to have installed an ivory-billed woodpecker as the villain of the [Bierstadt] piece.  This magnificent bird was recently <a href="http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/sci/tech/4493825.stm">sighted</a> in Arkansas after having been declared extinct in 1920.</p>
