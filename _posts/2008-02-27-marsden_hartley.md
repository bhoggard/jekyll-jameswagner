Marsden Hartley <em>Finnish-Yankee Wrestler</em> ca. 1938-1939 oil on board 24" x 18"</p>

<p>This small painting by <a href="/2007/07/brooklyn_museum_2.html">Marsden Hartley</a> was one of the reasons I headed up to the Park Avenue Armory on the last day of the <a href="http://www.artdealers.org/artshow.html">ADAA show</a> this past Monday, having been alerted to it by Karen Rosenberg's <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2008/02/22/arts/design/22armo.html?scp=1&sq=park+avenue+armory+show&st=nyt">article</a> in the <em>NYTimes</em> on Friday.</p>

<p>It was in the <a href="http://www.babcockgalleries.com/">Babcock Galleries</a> booth, imprisoned inside an embarassingly-humdrum, molded gold frame.  It did absolutely nothing for Hartley's subject, or his gentle love for the robust New England to which he had returned toward the end of his life.</p>
