Arthur Russell (and 'phones) in photo booth</p>

<p>Matt Wolf's beautiful homage to Arthur Russell, "Wild Combination", has been showing at the <a href="http://www.ifccenter.com/index">IFC Center </a>in the West Village, the former <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2003/07/15/movies/15WAVE.html?ex=1373601600&en=502bfc669f71cd10&ei=5007&partner=USERLAND">Waverly</a>, since September 26.  It also opened at the <a href="http://www.ica.org.uk/Wild%20Combination%3A%20A%20Portrait%20of%20Arthur%20Russell+17635.twl">ICA Cinema</a> in London that day.  In New York it's currently scheduled to be shown through next Tuesday, October 7, but it might be extended, depending upon attendance.   So go now.</p>

<p>It's a work of art, and a great joy.</p>

<p>I <a href="/2008/05/matt_wolfs_wild.html">wrote</a> about it after seeing the preview at The Kitchen last May.  There are a number on links, including a short sound widget, on my earlier post, but you probably won't want to miss the film.</p>
