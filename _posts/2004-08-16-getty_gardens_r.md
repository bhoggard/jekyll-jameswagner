<p><a href="http://www.pacewildenstein.com/jsp/artists/RobertIrwin.jsp">Robert Irwin</a>'s <a href="http://www.nctimes.net/~hihorse/page263.htm">Central Garden</a> at the Getty Center includes three magnificent Bougainvillea trellises assembled from concrete rebar alone.  Their shapes suggest small fanciful <a href="http://www.blueplanetbiomes.org/baobab.htm">Baobab</a> trees, and offer just about the same amount of shade to the benches arranged at their feet.</p>
