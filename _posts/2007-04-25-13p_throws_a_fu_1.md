<p>The feisty Obie-winning playwrights' collective <a href="http://bloggy.com/mt/archives/004117.html">13P </a> ("we don't develop plays.  we do them.") is having a cabaret benefit at <a href="http://web.joespub.com/web_joes/">Joe's Pub</a> this Sunday, April 29.   Estelle Parsons, Lisa Kron and James Urbaniak will be among the participants on stage.</p>

<p>Tickets are $100 and include reserved seating at the 7:00 pm cabaret, with access to a post-show reception and silent auction at 8:30.  There are more details are on their <a href="http://13p.org/">website</a>, but the thumbnail below is an image of one of the auction items, Philip Pearlstein's 1984 color etching with aquatint, "Nude on Bamboo".</p>
