<p>We're flying to Spain tomorrow for two weeks:  Madrid, a motor trip north, ending in Barcelona, from which we fly back on the 16th.</p>

<p>I was in Barcelona in 1961.  I was almost arrested for wearing Bermuda shorts while riding around on my bike.  I went back to Spain (Madrid, Toledo, Segovia, Salamanca) in 1980.  the Caudillo was dead, and it felt like one big party;  I can't believe I haven't been been back in the meantime.</p>

<p>Barry just did a <a href="http://bloggy.com/mt/archives/006253.html">post</a> covering some territory we're going to have to miss, several benefits and some theatre.</p>

<p>I have no idea whether I'll have a chance to blog while away.  Okay, maybe some pictures if we have good internet connections.</p>
