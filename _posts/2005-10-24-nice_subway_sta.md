<p>The beautiful red marble which covers the pillars, every wall and even the benches inside the <em>Mohrenstrasse</em> stop on the number 2 U-Bahn has an interesting heritage.  It once decorated the walls of Adolf Hitler's lordly <em>Reichskanzlei</em> [Imperial Chancellery], which had stood just a block away.  The DDR officials apparently saw no reason to waste resources while rebuilding the transit system after the war.</p>
