<p><a href="/2004/04/david_humphrey_2.html">David Humphrey</a> unveiled the delightful products of his latest trip into the fantastic on Friday evening.  His "Oven Stuffer Roaster" installation is now, well, <em>stuffed</em> into the courtyard of the <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/2/678">Morsel</a> gallery in (rather) far eastern Williamsburg.  The sculptures are essentially reconfigured giant (but still much smaller than Macy's) Thanksgiving turkey balloons here lit from within.</p>
