<p><a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/6/4771">Thrust Projects</a> is currently mounting an exhibition of work by <a href="/2007/04/matthew_lutz_ki_2.html">Matthew Lutz-Kinoy</a> and <a href="http://www.sophynaess.com/">Sophy Naess</a> titled, "darling! what color".  The artists, who are in fact good friends and who are both comfortable in a number of media, including performance, are represented here both individually and in collaboration, in beautiful work that stretches the idea of figurative representation but manages just as often to betray the indifference of pure abstraction.</p>
