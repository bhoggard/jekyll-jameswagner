<p>I captured a few, <em>too</em> few, scenes yesterday at "<a href="http://www.newartdealers.org/events/?id=75">NADA's County Affair</a>".   The good old hot time available on <a href="/2006/01/over_half_a_doz.html">West 27th Street</a> wasn't just the work of the weather:  If every art fair exhibited the kind of creativity, energy and fun at large (and small) on those ancient paving stones yesterday, the organizers would have to ration the public's access with prepaid time slots.</p>
