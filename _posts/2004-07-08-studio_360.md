<p>This Saturday the Public Radio show, "<a href="http://www.wnyc.org/studio360/">Studio 360</a>," will include a segment by <a href="http://www.scps.nyu.edu/faculty/index.jsp?deptId=26&insId=3606">Matthew Schuerman</a> on the "<a href="http://www.ericdoeringer.com/bootlegs.html">Bootlegs</a>" project of the artist, Eric Doeringer.</p>

<blockquote>
<p>“Good artists borrow; great artists steal” – the old saying has been attributed to Picasso but he may have stolen it as well. This week on Studio 360, Kurt Andersen and his guest, the writer and musician Greg Tate, talk about the artistic need to take other people’s work.</p>
</blockquote>

<p>We delight in collecting his work and <a href="http://www.bloggy.com/mt/">Barry</a> and I have both written about him in the past.  It's likely we will be heard in the edited sound picture, and I have the same concern expressed on Bloggy:  "I hope I didn't embarass myself too badly."</p>

<p>In New York, the program will air on 93.9 FM at 10 AM Saturday, July 10 and on 820 AM at 7 PM on Sunday, July 11. You can also listen online to <a href="http://www.wnyc.org/">WNYC</a>.</p>

<p>To find broadcast times/stations in other areas, you can visit <a href="http://www.wnyc.org/studio360/listings.html">Studio 360</a>.  The program will also be archived for one week after the broadcast <a href="http://www.wnyc.org/studio360/show.html">here</a> (after that you have to pay to listen).</p>
