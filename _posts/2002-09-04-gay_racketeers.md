<p>We love Paul Rudnick!  This week in <u>The</u> <u>New</u> <u>Yorker</u> he writes a helpful <a href="http://www.newyorker.com/printable/?shouts/020909sh_shouts">memo</a> to the FBI which should assist them in an investigation of the putative <a href="http://www.ew.com/ew/report/0,6115,269936~10~6~michaelovitzblameshis,00.html">Gay Mafia</a>.</p>

<blockquote>
<p>RE: The F.B.I.'s racketeering division recently infiltrated the nation's alleged Gay Mafia, with operatives working under cover as vicious choreographers, neo-con columnists, and chatty houseboys. These moles have discovered many significant differences between this far-reaching criminal enterprise and its heterosexual counterpart.</p>
</blockquote>

<p>7. The Gay Mafia's links to the Catholic Church are extensive, and most often begin with the phrase "Jimmy, did you know that the Apostles liked to wrestle?"</p>
