<p>Calvin Trillin in the <a href="http://www.thenation.com/">January 27 edition</a> of <u>The</u> <u>Nation</u>:</p>

<blockquote>
<p>THE QUESTION OF NORTH KOREA</p>

<p>Korea has the bomb, but not to worry.<br />
It's not a crisis.  No, we needn't hurry<br />
To get inspections back.  Why try to spot<br />
The weapons they already say they've got?<br />
Containment's fine, no reason for attack.<br />
The threat, we've said for months, is just Iraq.<br />
And why destroy Saddam but still contrive<br />
To let this wicked Kim Jong Il survive?<br />
Because one wicked tyrant must remain<br />
To run against in Bush's next campaign.</p>
</blockquote>
