<blockquote>
<p>As far as I'm concerned, the flawed idealism of Zionism has run up against a wall. Even if I accepted the biblical premise that Jews are entitled to that piece of Levantine real estate - and I don't - the political reality is that you cannot find peace by pursuing your current objectives. And you threaten more than yourselves and your immediate neighbors; you are threatening those of us who contributed so heavily to your existence. <br />
....<br />
In fact, if Israel insists on maintaining the occupation, I will take action. I will demand my trees back. You owe me.</p>
