<p><a href="http://www.johnconnellypresents.com/">John Connelly Presents</a> continues to balance shows in two separate gallery spaces on the tenth floor of 526 West 26th Street, and they're always eye-openers, even for the most jaded art <em>boulevardier</em>.  This month is no exception, with eleven abstract videos by Ara Peterson in one room, and Justin Samson's extravagant installation in his  reconstruction of the main space.</p>
