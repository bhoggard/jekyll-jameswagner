<p>The world which surrounded the early twentieth-century mystic <a href="http://www.gurdjieff.org/">G.I Gurdjieff </a>is only the starting point for a beautiful multi-media installation by Timothy Marvel Hull at Klaus von Nichtssagend.   This wonderful Williamsburg <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/2/3035">show</a> closes this Sunday, but for those who won't be able to get to Union Avenue there are some great images on the gallery <a href="http://www.klausgallery.com/index.html">website</a>.</p>
