<p>Ved Mehta, talking wistfully about <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2003/05/22/garden/22MEHT.html">the house</a> he did not get:"Really, I wanted to buy an old house and adapt to it," he said.</p>

<blockquote>
<p>In America, everyone thinks he can build his own dream house. In the rest of the world, you adapt to the house. This country is so rich, everyone wants to dominate the world, not adapt to it.</p>
</blockquote>
