<p>The <a href="https://jameswagner.com/index.php?m=200303#734">Iranian cyclist</a> arrested and held by our immigration authorities for about four months this winter is now in California and about to begin his last sprint to New York City, completing an odyssey of six years.</p>

<p>The short message below, which probably went to an enormous number of friends and supporters, doesn't indicate whether he will be running on foot, as he had earlier indicated he would, or on the two wheels which have carried him around the world.</p>

<blockquote>
<p>Hello everyone,</p>

<p>Everything is fine with me.  I left Phoenix on Sunday,<br />
April 27th and rode to Los Angeles and I am staying<br />
with a new friend, Dave Hyslop in Marina del Rey,<br />
California.</p>

<p>On May 11th (Mother's Day) I will start the final leg<br />
of my journey and travel to Ground Zero in New York<br />
City.</p>

<p>Your suport has meant so much to me - thank you!</p>

<p>Sincerely,</p>

<p>Reza Baluchi</p>
</blockquote>
