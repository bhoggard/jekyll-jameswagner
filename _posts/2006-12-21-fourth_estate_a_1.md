<p><a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/2/3621">Klaus von Nichtssagend</a> is showing eight print editions by eight different artists.  They were produced by a new Brooklyn shop called <a href="http://www.forthestate.com/">Forth Estate</a>, founded by master printer Luther Davis and [master] artist <a href="http://glenbaldridge.com/">Glen Baldridge</a>.  The show includes a huge range of styles or approaches and the works represent an equally diverse, and very ambitious, set of techniques and materials.  Each piece is as terrific as an object as it is as an image.</p>

<p>The artists are Bjorn Copeland, Elise Ferguson, Joseph Hart, Andrew Kuo, Tim Lokiec, Carter Mull, Ian Pedigo and Milton Rosa-Ortiz.</p>
