<p>I haven't seen <a href="http://www.cnn.com/2003/US/10/16/rumsfeld.boykin.ap/index.html">this</a> anywhere else yet, so it must be an April fool's joke.  But, wait,  this is October!</p>

<p>These people are definitely going to get us all killed!</p>

<blockquote>
<p>Defense Secretary Donald H. Rumsfeld and the chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff are defending a new deputy undersecretary of defense "who has reportedly cast the war on terror" in religious terms.</p>

<p>Lt. Gen. William G. Boykin, whose promotion and appointment was confirmed by the Senate in June, has said publicly that he sees the war on terrorism as a clash between Judeo-Christian values and Satan, the Los Angeles Times reported Thursday.</p>

<p>Appearing in dress uniform before a religious group in Oregon in June, Boykin said Islamic extremists hate the United States "because we're a Christian nation, because our foundation and our roots are Judeo-Christians. ... And the enemy is a guy named Satan."</p>

<p>. . . .</p>

<p>Discussing a U.S. Army battle against a Muslim warlord in Somalia in 1993, Boykin told one audience, "I knew my god was bigger than his. I knew that my god was a real god and his was an idol."</p>
</blockquote>

<p>Mine's bigger than yours?     He really said that?</p>

<p>Hey, this just in, spotted here at one in the morning.   The <u>NYTimes</u> has the <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2003/10/17/international/middleeast/17GENE.html?pagewanted=print&position=">story</a> in Friday's editions.  It's there that we finally learn how Bush became president.</p>

<blockquote>
<p>In another speech, General Boykin said God had selected George W. Bush as president. "Why is this man in the White House?" he asked. "The majority of Americans did not vote for him. Why is he there? And I tell you this morning that he's in the White House because God put him there for a time such as this."</p>
</blockquote>

<p>The <u>Times</u> tells us that he was he was wearing his military uniform and was speaking at religious functions each time he is reported to have delivered the messages now being reported.</p>
