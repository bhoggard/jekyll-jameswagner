<p>We really happy to spend some time with Teresa Moro today, especially since we weren't originally sure we would even find her while we were here.   We hadn't seen the artist since her solo show at <a href="http://www.foxyproduction.com/artist/view/16">Foxy Production</a> a little over three years ago, when we <a href="http://bloggy.com/mt/archives/003789.html">went home</a> with three beautiful  gouaches.</p>

<p>We had a terrific leisurely lunch in Malasaña, where we were joined by John Thomson of Foxy, who had helped us locate the artist here and who was in Madrid himself, representing New York's Elecronic Arts Intermix at a new media event at Circulo des Belles Artes.   After lunch, John went off to participate in a panel and Barry and I headed to Lavapiés with Teresa.  There we visited her Madrid gallery, <a href="http://www.mynameslolita.com/expobv.php">My name's Lolita Art</a>, and were shown a number of her newer drawings and paintings, including these two gorgeous acrylics on linen.</p>
