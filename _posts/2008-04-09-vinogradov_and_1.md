<p>Now that I've checked out the artists, and know something about what they're up to, my on-sight seduction at the Armory by this cutesy, sexy, pretty, porny, realisty, fairie and quite funny painting by Russians <a href="http://www.deitch.com/projects/sub.php?projId=33">Alexander Vinogradov and Vladimir Dubossarsky</a> makes better sense to me.  I love the fat flying penguins and the quite-thick-around-the-middle youth with the four rosy cheeks.  <a href="http://www.deitch.com/index.php">Deitch Projects</a> showed the painting.</p>

<p>I also noticed the title, and the date of the painting's completion.  Was it before or after that beautiful summer day "when our world changed forever"?</p>
