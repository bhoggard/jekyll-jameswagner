<p>Mary Boone has asked Jose Freire of <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/1/1805">Team</a> to curate a series of three shows at her uptown gallery.  The idiosyncrasies of the first installment "I Love My Scene" promise a continuing, very personal tour inside a very active mind.  In any event, the promise alone brought us into an uptown space for the first time in months.</p>

<p>The <a href="http://www.maryboonegallery.com/exhibitions/2005-2006/scene1/index.html">first installation</a> closes Saturday and includes a surprising group of works by <a href="http://www.antonkerngallery.com/artists/artists.php?artist=Hempel">Lothar Hempel</a> [in forground above], <a href="http://keithsonnierstudio.com/">Keith Sonnier</a> [in middle distance], Weegee [on wall], Cecil Beaton, <a href="http://www.heraldst.com/artists/bronstein.html">Pablo Bronstein</a> and <a href="/2004/11/group_team.html">Banks Violette</a>.</p>
