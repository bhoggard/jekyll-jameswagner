<p>The brilliant downtown team of <a href="/2002/09/kiki_and_herb_k.html">Kiki & Herb</a> has taken a summer share on Broadway.   I've been a huge fan for years (of these two, not Broadway), so although I haven't seen the show which opened last night, I have no doubts that the increasingly <em>really</em> silly "Great White Way" is embarassed this morning - embarassed about the fare being proffered to the tourists in most of its other theaters.</p>

<p>Justin Bond and Kenny Mellman are incredible artists, and still much, much more.</p>

<p>Choire Siccha has a terrific <a href="http://observer.com/20060814/20060814_Choire_Sicha_culture_newsstory1.asp">piece</a> written before the show opened and <a href="http://theater.nytimes.com/pages/theater/index.html">Ben Brantley</a> doesn't seem to have enough good things to say about what he saw from the aisle last night.    <strong>NOTE:</strong>  The <u>NYTimes</u> site also has an audio and slide show, but you really have to be there.</p>
