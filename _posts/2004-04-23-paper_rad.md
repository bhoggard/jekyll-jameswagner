<p>More group life from Providence, Rhode Island, even if these three guys are now sowing their art all about the country.</p>

<p><a href="http://www.foxyproduction.com/default.htm">Foxy Production</a> is currently showing the work of the collaborative <a href="http://www.paperrad.org/">Paper Rad</a>, composed of of Jacob Ciocci, Jessica Ciocci and Benjamin Jones.   Look for gorgeous paper, paint, video, cloth, music and sculpture, including familiar icons reimagined and everything invested with smart good humor.  Don't miss the treasure chest in the back room.</p>
