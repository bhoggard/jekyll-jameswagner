<p>On Friday I saw this piece by <a href="http://www.moma.org/collection/browse_results.php?object_id=79255">Ron Davis</a> hovering over the information/members desk at the Museum of Modern Art.  Delicious.  Looking like nothing so much as a shiny, candy-colored flying saucer, it was love at first sight, and I completely forgot what we had come up to ask for.  Fortunately Barry was much more focused, and we quickly got the day passes we wanted for our three guests.</p>
