<p>I saw these three Dan Rushton paintings at <a href="http://www.art-212.com/">ART (212)</a>.  I know the fair is long gone, but these images  aren't, and the artist responsible for them is expected to have a solo show this year at <a href="http://www.motihasson.com/">Moti Hasson</a>.  They will grace that gallery's new large, ground-floor space in Chelsea.</p>

<p>If you have been somewhat familiar with Rushton's painting <a href="/2004/09/dan_rushton_rev.html">up to now</a>, you'll have to agree this new work is clearly on a "hole nuba lebel"*.</p>
