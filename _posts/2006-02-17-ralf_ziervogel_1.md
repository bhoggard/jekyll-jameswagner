<p>A new (but not really so surprising) addition to the huge Chelsea <a href="http://www.artcal.net/">gallery list</a> appeared on 19th Street last night.  The young German curator opened his shop with a show of work by the Berliner <a href="http://www.artnews.info/ralfziervogel/">Ralf Ziervogel</a>.</p>

<p>Schlechtriem slaps a "temporary" sticker on all his announcements because, he says, he doesn't know how long he really wants to be a gallerist, but he says he'll give it at least a year.</p>

<p>Unfortunately we had to leave early this time in order to make an opera curtain <em>on the Lower East Side</em> [yup], but on the basis of the quality of the drawings I saw last night I'll definitely be back at least as often as Schlechtriem decides to keep this thing going.</p>
