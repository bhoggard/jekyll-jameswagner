<p>I was already a fan of the work of <a href="/2005/03/art_rock.html">Richard Aldrich</a> and <a href="/2006/09/alice_koenitz_a_1.html">Alice Könitz</a>, but based on what I saw last Friday I'm now just as enthusiastic about Los Angeles-based <a href="http://www.tellesfineart.com/nathanhylden.html">Nathan Hylden</a> and Glaswegian <a href="http://www.suttonlane.com/artist.php?a=mw&p=home">Michael WIlkinson</a>, the other two artists in the elegant current installation at Wallspace, "<a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/1/5076">Laying Bricks</a>".    Writer and critic <a href="http://michaelnedholte.com/">Michael Ned Holte</a> is the curator.</p>

<p>The show is a beauty, but it will be there for just two more days.</p>
