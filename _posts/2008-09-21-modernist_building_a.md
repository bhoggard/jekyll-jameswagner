<p>Earlier this evening I spotted this canvas leaning against a light pole on Metropolitan Avenue near the BQE overpass.  It's a painting of a modernist steel and glass building, and it's been carefully pressed into the shape of the architectural image it outlines.</p>

<p>I like it as sculpture, especially with that broken lower stretcher rail which added a bend to the right of the center.  Maybe it's still there.</p>
