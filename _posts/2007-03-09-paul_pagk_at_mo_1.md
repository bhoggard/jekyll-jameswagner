<p><a href="http://www.motihasson.com/">Moti Hasson</a> was showing a number of gallery artists in their booth at the <a href="http://www.scope-art.com/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=84&Itemid=251">Scope</a> fair, but as <a href="http://www.artnet.com/Magazine/people/smith/smith5-2-6.asp">Paul Pagk</a> hasn't yet shown up on these pages and I haven't yet seen his <a href="http://www.motihasson.com/exhibitions/current.html">current solo show</a> in the gallery, I was anxious to show these two paintings now.</p>

<p>I have however seen enough of his work elsewhere to confirm that they are always even more beautiful than these images suggest.</p>
