<p>As if the news from the past week wasn't sufficiently horrific already, we have to prepare ourselves for what still lies ahead.</p>

<p>I just saw a headline expressing alarm about what lies <a href="http://news.yahoo.com/news?tmpl=story&cid=514&u=/ap/20050906/ap_on_re_us/hurricane_katrina_22">beneath the water</a>.  But the accompanying story is about much more than the bodies of people who have already succumbed to this natural and man-made disaster.  The water itself holds still more peril for the entire Gulf region.  <a href="http://www.polizeros.com/categories/thePoliticsOfWater/">This site</a> has been doing an excellent job preparing us for the news we will be seeing for many years to come.</p>

<p>The real disaster may have only just begun.</p>
