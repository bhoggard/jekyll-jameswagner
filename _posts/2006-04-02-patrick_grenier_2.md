<p>Patrick Grenier, and certainly everyone else who makes it to Freeman Alley this month, is enjoying his second solo show at <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/6/2123">Silo</a>.  Grenier <a href="/2005/01/patrick_grenier.html">remains</a> concerned with the (corrupting) relationship between the artist and the public venue of the art, with a particular interest in architecture.</p>

<p>The exhibition is definitely not the dry exercise the premise might suggest.  The second image above is of an installation which includes a videotaped documentation of a "showdown between replicas of newly-canonized structures, such as the Walker Art Center addition in Minneapolis by the Herzog & de Meuron and Gehry's Guggenheim Bilbao."</p>
