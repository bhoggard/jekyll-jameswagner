<p>Sterling Ruby is <a href="https://jameswagner.com/mt/mt-search.cgi?IncludeBlogs=4&search=sterling+ruby">back</a> at Foxy Production and once again he's all over the place in both the <a href="http://www.foxyproduction.com/Exhibitions.html">solo show</a> as a whole and in the individual works that each refuse to be limited to a single medium themselves.  Collage seems to dominate new work in the visual arts everywhere lately, but Ruby's entire oeuvre can be understood as a single great collage of inventions, and each one of them in turn collages his bold, complex understanding of his world in layers which reveal little but promise much.</p>

<p>I just wish I could take it all home where I might see just how far I could get with it.</p>
