<p>Horrible to know not only that we can't expect intelligence or competence from the Bushies, but do we also have to be frightened out of our wits in the process?  Can't Chicken Little at least pretend to have a little dignity?  Read the first <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2002/05/23/opinion/L23TERR.html">letter</a> in today's NYTimes.</p>

<blockquote>
<p>Making blanket statements about the inevitable success of terrorism in the United States plays right into the hands of the enemy and contributes to public hysteria. <br />
Instilling fear in a population is a major objective of every terrorist cell.</p>
</blockquote>

<p>The writer then offers his suggested outline of the message a government ought to send in these circumstances.</p>
