<p>The <a href="http://www.theonion.com/onion3816/god_re-floods_middle_east.html">Onion</a> reports this week, in banner headline, "God Re-Floods Middle East."</p>

<blockquote>
<p>JERUSALEM—In what theological and meteorological authorities are calling "a wrathful display of Old Testament proportions," the Lord Almighty re-flooded the Middle East Tuesday, making good on last week's threat to wipe the region clean if there was not an immediate halt to the bloodshed between Arabs and Jews.</p>
