<p>Reno, now more than ever.</p>

<p>The NYTimes visits Reno's new show and Anita Gates <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2002/05/07/arts/theater/07RENO.html?pagewanted=print">finds it </a>". . . consistently, energetically, loudly funny."</p>

<blockquote>
<p>Reno sticks to her politics, no matter what the current fashion. At a recent performance her anti-Giuliani comments met with nervous silence, but her anti-Bush remarks were a hit. "Can't we get a smart guy?" she asks at one point, describing the president's unrehearsed public-speaking style as "like a drunk trying to look sober."</p>
</blockquote>
