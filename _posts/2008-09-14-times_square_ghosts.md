<p>Early last Saturday we met up with a visiting friend who had never been to New York before, so of course we didn't let the rain keep us inside.  We ended up walking all over Manhattan.   In the midst of the worst of it I found this handsome family of ghosts sheltering, like the three of us, under a marquee on Broadway in Times Square.</p>
