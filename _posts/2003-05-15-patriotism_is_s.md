<blockquote>
<p>Patriotism in its simplest, clearest, and most indubitable meaning is nothing but an instrument <br />
for the attainment of the government's ambitious and mercenary aims, <br />
and a renunciation of human dignity, common sense, and conscience by the governed, <br />
and a slavish submission to those who hold power. <br />
That is what is really preached wherever patriotism is championed. <br />
Patriotism is slavery.</p>

<p>- Leo Tolstoi, <i>Christianity and Patriotism</i> (1894)</p>
</blockquote>
