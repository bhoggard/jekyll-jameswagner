<p><a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/1/1070">"I'm a child of divorce, Gimme a break"</a> is the rummy title of the current show at Cynthia Broan.  The artists and the works were chosen for their affinity with the artmaking of the curator himself, Michael St. John.  Not surprisingly, given its particularly ebullient character, all of them seem to be having a good time, and this writer was almost all smiles as he shared in it.</p>

<p>A very good, smart show, with no redundancies in spite of the risks of its conceptualization.</p>
