<p>It takes more than epithets to deal with complex problems.  It never hurts to do some thinking.  Sharon is almost an open book these days, but many of us want to know why we have such a <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2002/05/10/opinion/10ANDE.html">problem with Arafat</a>?</p>

<blockquote>
<blockquote>
<p>What is it about Yasir Arafat that makes him so troubling to the United States and Israel? In fact, the problem is not the man himself, as Mr. Sharon and President Bush seem to think, but the political position of the Palestinians in the Arab world today.</p>
</blockquote>
