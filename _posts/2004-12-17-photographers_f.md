<p>REMINDER:  Don't miss being a part of the photographers' <a href="/2004/12/fight_the_propo.html">'Flash Mob'</a> subway ride protest against the MTA proposal to ban all cameras from the entire transit system.  The organizers' plan is to meet tomorrow, Saturday, at 1 o'clock in the awesome Main Concourse inside <a href="http://www.grandcentralterminal.com/">Grand Central Terminal</a>.   Don't forget your metro card and your camera.  Bring a sign with a creative message, even a small one.</p>

<p>The magnificent Concourse is worth a picture even without the addition of hundreds of concerned young camera fanatics, and if the MTA has its way, this will be one of your last chances to record its spendors.</p>
