<p>It was pretty quiet this afternoon around <a href="http://www.thevillager.com/villager_22/pier57construction.html">Marine & Aviation Pier 57</a>, where nearly 2000 people were arrested and detained this week while they were exercising freedoms they imagined might be protected by the American legal system, or, in some cases, just because they happened to be near the police at the wrong moment.  The only activity visible today was that of workmen collecting the countless port-a-sans which had been set up inside each of the filthy metal holding pens inside.  We didn't get too close.</p>
