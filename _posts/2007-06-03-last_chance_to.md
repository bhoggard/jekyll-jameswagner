<p>Sharp viewer/readers will have noticed by now that an advert has appeared on my site for the first time, on the upper left corner of each page.</p>

<p>I thought that if I were ever to start this sort of thing, this would be the perfect time to break a five-year tradition of private publishing.</p>

<p>The spot is for NURTUREart's <a href="/2007/05/nurtureart_bene_1.html">benefit</a> tomorrow evening, and yes, Barry and I are being honored at the occasion.  It's all a bit embarassing, and to mention it again would be even more embarrassing if the institution wasn't such a great cause.</p>

<p>There are still tickets available, starting at $75, and the event is conveniently located in the Chelsea gallery district (although I imagine many Williamsburgers will argue about the convenience of Chelsea).</p>

<p>Hope to see you there.</p>
