<p>In spite of their seriousness and the pain they evoke, the humor and compassion of <a href="http://www.perjovschi.ro/">Dan Perjovschi</a>'s delicate childlike drawings in the atrium at the Museum of Modern Art are totally infectious and, perhaps surprisingly, a great use of this huge space.  It's interesting that the gargantuan works of Richard Serra are installed elsewhere in the building.</p>

<p>Barry loves the "no smoking" symbol on the tank turret.</p>

<p>Perjovschi's entire piece is titled, "What Happened to US?", touching on responsibilities both personal and corporate.  I'm very sorry we missed the two-week performance, his <a href="Perjovschi">installation</a> of the work.</p>
