<p>There were a number of interesting gallery openings in Chelsea and elsewhere on January 11.  We had tickets for a 7:30 <a href="/2007/01/euripedes_becom_1.html">performance</a> in SoHo, but we still might have been able to make a number of shows before heading further downtown.  We decided instead to visit perhaps the least obvious opening, that for a show called "<a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/3/3826">Every Last Day</a>", at the current, storefront location of <a href="http://www.chashama.org/home.php">Chashama</a>, just off Times Square.  We weren't disappointed.</p>

<p>The exhibition was mounted by an expanding collective of inventive artists called <a href="http://www.dospestaneos.com/">Dos Pestañeos</a>.   The show is called "Every Last Day".   I want and expect to see more from these people, whether together or otherwise.</p>

<p>The <em>last day</em> of the show is February 28.</p>
