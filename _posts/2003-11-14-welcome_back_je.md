the new, temporary WTC PATH terminus, still behind chain link fencing on November 13th, 2003</p>

<p>The path is about to be <a href="http://www.guardian.co.uk/uslatest/story/0,1282,-3331785,00.html">reopened</a>.</p>

<p>On November 23rd a PATH train will be pulling up at the site of the World Trade Center, the first in over two years.  It will be the very same train of eight cars which pulled out of the station on September 11, 2001, minutes after the Towers were hit, rescuing all of its passengers.</p>

<p>The image shows that the identifying sign is still being assembled.</p>
