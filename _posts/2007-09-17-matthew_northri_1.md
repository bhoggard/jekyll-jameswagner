<p>I have to admit that among the changing installations in the Chelsea gallery area I look forward to most, with something like the same childish delight with which I once welcomed Tooth Fairy or Easter Bunny visits, are those to be found in <a href="http://www.zachfeuer.com/">Zach Feuer</a>'s <a href="/2007/04/the_art_of_the_1.html">window on 10th Avenue</a> (the west side, between 25th and 26th Street).  This modest space, which has been carved out of a former entryway, is currently occupied by a single wonderful piece by <a href="http://matthewnorthridge.com/">Matthew Northridge</a>.</p>
