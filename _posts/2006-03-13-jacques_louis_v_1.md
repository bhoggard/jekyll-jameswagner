<p>At Scope this week D.C.'s <a href="http://www.curatorsoffice.com/">Curator's Office</a> gallery exhibited some delightful 3-4 minute videos by Jacques Louis Vidal,  along with one very good drawing.  For the performance videos think of a slightly more serious John Bock collaborating with an almost wacky Chris Burden.</p>
