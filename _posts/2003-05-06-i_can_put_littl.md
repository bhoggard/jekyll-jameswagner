<p><a href="http://www.motherjones.com/mother_jones/JA95/bernstein.html">Tony Kushner</a> and Mark Harris met five years ago.  Last month they <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2003/05/04/fashion/weddings/04VOWS.html">affirmed their partnership</a> before friends in Manhattan.  The occasion made the "<b>VOWS</b>" feature in the <u>NYTimes</u> Styles section this past Sunday.</p>

<blockquote>
<p>They started planning their commitment ceremony soon after 9/11, buying rings at Tiffany's and complementary gray-toned suits at Saks.</p>

<p>The sales clerks were all accommodating, they said.  "One cake designer I called said, 'We specialize in elaborate beautiful white flowers all over the cake,'" Mr. Harris recalled.  "So I said, 'I should tell you, this is for two men.'  There was a slight pause and she said, `I can put little baseball players all over it?'"</p>
</blockquote>
