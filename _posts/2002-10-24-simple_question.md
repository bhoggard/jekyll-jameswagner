<p>What does the world look like today, compared to what it looked like just two years ago?</p>

<p>No, the dwarf in the White House can't be blamed for everything, but he certainly can't be credited with anything.</p>

<p>Admittedly he has done great things for his donors, precisely the people who don't pay serious taxes, only serious gratuities.  Wow, have they gotten good service!</p>

<p>But why on earth are we being told how extraordinarily "popular" he is?  I'm afraid to think too much about an answer to that question.</p>

<p>Oh, and has anyone seen a non-war issue lying about lately?  I guess, since there's still a full week and a half before the election, there's still plenty of time.  That is, unless sometime in the next days we see our national sniper standing behind the presidential seal to make a very serious announcement.</p>

<p>We're probably already doomed, and the world with us, unfortunately.</p>
