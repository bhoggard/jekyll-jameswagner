<p>Geesh, these people must think we're all really stupid!</p>

<p>New York Republican neanderthals have finally <a href="http://www.nydailynews.com/news/politics/story/29373p-27895c.html">decided</a> to let the New York Senate vote on gay rights, but only in order to secure more votes for Republican candidates in November.</p>

<p>The Democratic candidate for governor said it best.</p>

<blockquote>
<p>Asked about [Senate Majority Leader Joe] Bruno's promise, [Carl] McCall replied, "If that's true, these people really have no principles, do they?"</p>

<p>"For eight years, George Pataki has been promising it, Joe Bruno has been stopping it," said McCall, the state controller. "Now, what, two weeks before an election ... all of a sudden Joe Bruno and George Pataki come up with a little gimmick to try to buy off an endorsement? Most of the endorsements they have, they bought."</p>
</blockquote>

<p>But we shouldn't get too excited yet, since they are telling us that the vote will come <i>after</i> the November elections.</p>
