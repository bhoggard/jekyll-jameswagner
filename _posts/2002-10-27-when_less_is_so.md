<p>NEWLY OUT GAY MAN OVERDOING IT</p>

<p>STARVING THIRD WORLD MASSES WARNED AGAINST EVILS OF CONTRACEPTION</p>

<p>AMERICAN PEOPLE SHRUG, LINE UP FOR FINGERPRINTING</p>

<p>DEFENSE DEPARTMENT TYPO RESULTS IN U.S. ATTACK ON IRA</p>

<p>LINEBACKER FACES SUSPENSION FOR GENOCIDE</p>
</blockquote>

<p>Well, you get the idea.  If you don't, you probably aren't reading this weblog.</p>
