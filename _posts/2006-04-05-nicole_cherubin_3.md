<p>I can't promise to upload a big bunch of pictures for just any gallery which happens to have none on its own site, but this is a very special circumstance.  I'm very fond both of <a href="/2005/01/nicole_cherubin.html">Nicole Cherubini</a>'s sculpture and the wonderful work always to be found at <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/1/2201">Klemens Gasser & Tanja Grunert</a>, but the happy combination, as seen in last night's opening, is sensational.</p>

<p>Cherubini has re-invented pottery, making it finally (again?) indisputably safe for art.  This is very serious sculpture but with a fundamental good humor which, for anyone who experiences its beauty, can be as forward or latent as needed.</p>

<p>I have no list of the individual pieces, so the images I'm showing will have to go undoumented, at least for now.  I doubt these strong, glorious pots will mind that one bit.</p>
