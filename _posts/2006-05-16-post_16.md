<p>Very beautiful and lots of fun, the four large, gently-kinetic sculptures Olafur ELiasson has installed at <a href="http://www.tanyabonakdargallery.com/index.php">Tanya Bonakdar</a> do not hide their magic:  The visitor is invited to pay attention to the wizard behind the curtain.</p>
