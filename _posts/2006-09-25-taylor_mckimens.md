<p><a href="/2005/03/art_rock.html">Taylor McKimens</a> has a single large piece installed inside <a href="http://www.artcal.net/event/view/1/3028">Clementine Gallery.</a></p>

<blockquote>
<p>An exhibition featuring Taylor McKimens's first comic book, published by <a href="http://www.pictureboxinc.com/">PictureBox</a>. All of the artwork used to make the comic book has been re-mixed to make a single freestanding large scale artwork in the space.</p>
</blockquote>

<p>No old board fence ever looked so pretty - or so wonderfully yucky.   The show's title, "Drips", only begins to explain the latter;  the two details of the untitled 2006 work shown above should also help if you haven't been there yet.</p>

<p>Barry and I bought the comic - and the editioned small print - on the spot.  We both find it hard to stay away from McKimens.</p>
