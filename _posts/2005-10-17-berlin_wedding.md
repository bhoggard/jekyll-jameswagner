<p>It was the funniest wedding I've ever been to.</p>

<p>Dan said it certainly must be the most documented.  Everyone had a camera and half of those were in video mode.  One hour later, at the lunch following the ceremony, Laurence presented each of the grooms with a CD including hundreds of his raw stills alone.  I think the presentation itself was documented.</p>

<p>We enjoyed the day in the company of wonderful people in a wondrous mood in a wonder of a city.  Tonight we wish them all good fortune.</p>
