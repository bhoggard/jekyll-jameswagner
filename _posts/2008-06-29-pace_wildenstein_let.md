<p>They have no idea what they're talking about.  Once one of the most important galleries in the country, sadly Pace Wildenstein doesn't seem to know the first thing about the usage of photography in the twenty-first century, or the issue of <a href="/2008/01/more_re_photo_p_1.html">copyright</a>.</p>

<p>Because of the gallery's photo ban, Pace shows are not posted or reviewed on <a href="http://www.artcal.net/">ArtCal</a>, and they do not appear on this site or on <a href="http://bloggy.com/">Bloggy</a>.</p>
