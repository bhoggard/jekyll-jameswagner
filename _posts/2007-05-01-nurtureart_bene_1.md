<p>Barry has all the words, and he links to all the important information on his <a href="http://bloggy.com/mt/archives/006249.html">post</a>:  Williamsburg's NUTUREart is having a benefit party, and they're throwing it in Manhattan.  I suppose the location may have been chosen partly for the same reasons presidential candidates come to Manhattan, but CUE Art Foundation also has more room for <em>all the people I would expect are going to want to be there</em>.</p>

<p>It's a very good thing for emerging artists, and those who love them.</p>

<p>Barry and I would be excited about this event even if we weren't the honorees, and with <a href="http://www.williampowhida.com/powhida_final/index.htm">William Powhida</a> doing the introductions, it would seem risky to stay away no matter whom he was asked to talk about.</p>

<p>We're told tickets may be purchased on the <a href="http://www.nurtureart.org/">website</a> beginning Wednesday.</p>
