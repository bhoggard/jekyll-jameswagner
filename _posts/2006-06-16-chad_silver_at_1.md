<p>My apologies to anyone who ran into me last night at the <a href="http://www.aafnyc.com/">Affordable Art Fair</a> reception:  I was feeling a bit indisposed and I suspect I came off distracted at best.</p>

<p>I don't remember much but there are at least a few images I would have retained even if I hadn't been able to get my camera to capture them digitally.  One of the works I would like to engage under better circumstances is this 4-minute sound video by <a href="http://www.aafnyc.com/dynamic/artist.asp?ArtistID=3388">Chad Silver</a> shown by Gallery Boreas.  The anxious young man pictured is listening to a disembodied voice whose aberrant yet harmless suggestion he eventually adopts shortly before the credits roll.</p>

<p>The Fair continues through Sunday.</p>
