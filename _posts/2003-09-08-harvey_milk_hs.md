<p>Barry and I joined hundreds of others outside <a href="https://jameswagner.com/index.php?m=200309#1078">Harvey Milk High School</a> this morning to cheer students entering the building for the first day of classes.</p>

<p>Across the street was the Fred Phelps family circus of Christianist nuts.  The police seem to have understood the difference, and this time their sympathies worked for the good guys.  Supporters were assigned the sidewalk in front of the school on Astor Place.  The anti's had to be content with, appropriately, the gutter on the north side, and their screaming was continuously assaulted by the roar of a huge garbage/trash truck adjacent.  Who had arranged that intervention?</p>

<p>Their posters alone alienated the cops, as did the sight of several of them standing on or dragging the flag through the dirt, but it was when they pulled out the "THANK GOD FOR SEPT 11" sign, that the police barricade we were standing behind was immediately moved well into the street to give our crowd more comfort, and additional public visibility in the process.</p>

<p>The kids themselves are alright, and we'll be back this afternoon before 3, to see them safely out.</p>
