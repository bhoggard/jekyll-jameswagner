<p>A beautiful idyllic town, designed to please the biped who enjoys leisure, Portland nevertheless must be paid for.  These men however don't think the business has to be endured with hairshirts.</p>

<p>While they were having lunch at 12:30 in the afternoon, I was really enjoying the concept of leisure by making it brunch.  The scene was the very pleasant French cafe, Carafe, on shady SW Market St.</p>
