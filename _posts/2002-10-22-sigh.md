<p>It's a fucked-up country.</p>

<p>Sexphobia and religion encourages AIDS</p>

<p>Ever bigger, tanklike SUVs protect us from each other</p>

<p>Bombing is regularly employed to maintain peace</p>

<p>Health care is made a commodity</p>

<p>Incumbents are honored when terror strikes on their watch</p>

<p>The environment is converted into theme parks</p>

<p>Public schools must be supported by donations</p>

<p>--or replaced by private schools</p>

<p>History has been turned into a costume party</p>

<p>Violence is conventional</p>

<p>Drugs are regarded as more dangerous than a drug war</p>

<p>Democracy is rejected in favor of gossip</p>

<p>Populism is regarded as class war</p>

<p>Guns are absolutely the law of the land</p>

<p>Taxes are a duty for all but big business and the rich</p>

<p>Intelligence is always suspect</p>

<p>Corporations abjure tax obligations</p>

<p>--but compete to throw money at government</p>

<p>Theocracy is regarded as the ideal polity</p>

<p>Suburban villas keep us as far from each other as money can manage</p>

<p>Using public transportation is regarded as shameful</p>

<p>Morality must always be religious morality, and it usually means sex</p>

<p>Almost everything means sex</p>
