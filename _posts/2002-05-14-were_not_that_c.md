<p>Still <i>two</i> countries, Georgie!</p>

<p>Would it be better to believe the Unelected One was simply under a misapprehension, or just generally stupid?  Here are his exact words, as heard in news broadcasts, and as <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2002/05/14/international/europe/14PTEX.html">printed</a> in the NYTimes today:</p>

<blockquote>
<p>"Today, I'm pleased to announce that the United States and Russia has [sic] agreed to a treaty which will substantially reduce our nuclear arsenals to the agreed-upon range of 1700 to 2200 warheads."</p>
</blockquote>

<p>The "sic" is my own, and was not present in the Times text, unfortunately.</p>
