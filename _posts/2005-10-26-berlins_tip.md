<p>It's a very complicated site for me.  It's how we can find our way back to our apartment from anywhere in the city, it's where we joined our friend Dan for his sorta-rehearsal lunch soon after we arrived, it's the highest thing in the city, it was a point of pride for a people who had so little in material things to get excited about, it's kind of outrageous, especially down around the base of the tower, it's a wonderful retro entertainment machine, and, all in all, it's kinda neat.  It's the <a href="http://www.berlinerfernsehturm.de/engl/05home_e.html"><em>Fernsehturm</em></a> above Alexanderplatz, as seen at dusk tonight.</p>
