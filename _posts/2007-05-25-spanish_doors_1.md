<p>I fell in love with many of the older doors (and their portals) in Spain, both for their beauty and for the physical scars which mark their survival.  In some of the more ancient examples the wood had been replaced at some point and the original hardware re-installed.</p>

<p>The first three images were taken in Madrid, and the last was in the wall of one of the structures which surrounds the monastery/palace El Escorial.</p>
