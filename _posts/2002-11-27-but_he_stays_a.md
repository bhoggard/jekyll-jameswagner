<p>Canadian Prime Minister Jean Chrétien's aide has finally been forced to <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2002/11/27/international/americas/27CANA.html">resign</a> over her calling George W. Bush a "moron."</p>

<blockquote>
<p>Mr. Chrétien comes from the left wing of Canada's governing Liberals and is uncomfortable with Mr. Bush's stance on many issues, including the threats to attack Iraq.</p>

<p>Mr. Chrétien, 68, developed a close friendship with President Clinton during his presidency and often golfed with him. But he is one of the few leaders of a close United States ally yet to be invited to spend time at Mr. Bush's private ranch in Texas.</p>
</blockquote>

<p>Wow.  I think Chrétien probably knows how lucky he's been.</p>
